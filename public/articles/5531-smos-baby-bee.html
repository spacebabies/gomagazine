Smos & Baby Bee

Smos and Baby Bee are a duo who started their DJ career in early ‘90s, when they were amongst very few DJs who started to play house music on Belgian nightlife scene. Ze spelen Saturday 24 November op Jimpster.

They made their DJ name famous thanks to their funky, uplifting style, which combined percussive, tribal rhythms, UK and USA house with some disco and funk classics.

They became residents at Café D’Anvers’ in 1993, where they also played for Paradise’ and Natural elements’ from Patrick Socks and at many other parties which were organized at Café D’Anvers’. In 2003 they were celebrating 10 years residency anniversary and you can still hear them playing there every second Saturday in a month, when they perform their famous long sets.

Since 1998 until 2001 they were residents at Eskimo’ parties in Ghent, where they did their ‘trade mark’ closing sets in the house room and later on they also DJ-ed at many special parties which were organised by the same promoters ( Belmondo, Diesel U Music, at ‘69’ club in Ghent, ‘Culture club’ ... ). In 2004 they released a maxi ‘Don’t stop’ for Eskimo recording, under pseudonym Elastix.

Between January 2001 and October 2003 they were residents in Motion room in Fuse’ club in Brussels, where they still occasionally play as guest DJs.

From October 2003 until September 2004 they played as residents for concept ‘Colors’, in ‘Tijuana’ club in Ghent. They also played as residents for Le Club’ in Lille.

They played on many occasions as guest DJs in all good Belgian house clubs and many times abroad and so far they shared the decks with plenty of famous international DJ stars ( Kenny Hawkes, Silicone soul, Mark Farina, D’Julz, Free Form Five, Derrick Carter, Luke Solomon, Johny Fiasco, Tiefschwartz,... ). They’ve been also invited to play at some of the biggest summer festivals in Belgium.
