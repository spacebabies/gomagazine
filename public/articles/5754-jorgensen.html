Jørgensen

Jørgensen, half Danish half Dutch, has built up a steady reputation spinning rare deep house grooves with a latin touch. Speelt Saturday 23 February op In Vuur En Vlam.

Filling every gap on the dance floor he knows how to please women with his warm and soulful touch of sexy and smooth rhythms. As initiator of many events since late 90’s and his respected studio work this talent absolutely knows what he’s up to!

Style Jørgensen is a good example how to create a perfect ambiance for a good ‘mood party’. To stick to genres Jørgensen plays house music. From deep house to groovy or funky electrorated house and from tech house to original Chicago sound or UK garage. He rather build up a night bringing every musical influences in electronic music then represent one specific genre. As long as the dance floors are filled up, no one will blame him for that�?�!

National-highlights He played in several well known clubs, most of them in the south of Holland like Nightlive, 5611, Chateau d’House festival, Mezzo, Kings, W2, Groene Engel, but also in De Lichtfabriek, Paard van Troje, Club NL, Simplon, Cineac and did radio shows at DancenetFM, DanceRadioOne, Top Radio (Brussels- Belgium), D-Tektion (Geraardsbergen-Belgium).

International-highlights Spain, Poland, Belgium.

Played-with Kevin Yost, Ryan Dent, Barth Hendrix, Marcello, Marcella, Erick E, Benny Rodrigues, Bart van Liefland, Ricky Rivaro, Cor Fijneman, Ton TB and Marnix.

Discography Jørgensen has produced many tracks over the last years. Due to his own criticism and perfection it is not earlier then in 2005 that he will be ready to release his first EP. At the moment he is co producing with other Dutch producers like Bart Hendrix.
