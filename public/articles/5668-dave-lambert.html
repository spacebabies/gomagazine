Dave Lambert

Dave Lambert started out in 1990 with his own party concept in PARADOX (Antwerp/Belgium). Speelt Saturday 12 January op Versuz on Saturnights.

Dave Lambert started out in 1990 with his own party concept in PARADOX (Antwerp/Belgium). The events he started out with were merely focused on rock music! Back in the early days he played music from bands such as The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Biohazard, Pearl Jam and many more. As he got older he evolved into a more underground house style in clubs around Antwerp Belgium. It was at one of these nights he got spotted by the owner of a place called LE BEAU ZOO (1998) and was immediately asked to come and play on a regular basis. Right from the start he created a special vibe which let to enormous respect from famous Djs such as Marko (La Rocca), Pippi (Ibiza), and Oliver (Amnesia Ibiza). This also let to the creation of his first mixed compilations for Le Beau Zoo, Delano and Chill Music by Coca Cola!
