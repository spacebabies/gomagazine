Adultnapper

Adultnapper (Francis Harris), a NYC based producer/DJ, has played all over the world. Speelt zaterdag 29 september op Rex.

He has also been DJing in all the mega-clubs and producing big hits along with (such as ‘Digital Love’) with the likes of Mr. C and Number One Son. His sound can best be described as climaxing minimal techno with electro-house influences.
