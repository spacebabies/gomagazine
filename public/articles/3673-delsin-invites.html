Delsin invites Vince Watson in Wilhelmina

Delsin Invites.. Original Detroit Techno / 100% Technosoul with special live performance: Vince Watson (headspace, bio, f-com, transmat) and special guest deejay: Tom Churchill (headspace, emoticon). deejays: Peel Seamus and M>O>S/Aroy Dee.

Headspace is a soulful techno label run & done by Tom Churchill from Glasgow. Headspace just celebrated its 17th release in April with its first artist album ‘Sublimina’, courtesy of Scottish techno stalwart Vince Watson. Vince has been producing his highly personal brand of techno since the mid-90s, making his debut on Dave Angel?s rotation imprint and going on to record for many of the world?s biggest techno and house labels: Alola, Ibadan, Soma, Pure Plastic, to name but a handful.

Vince must be one of the hardest-working producers in the UK at the moment, with releases out now or coming soon on his own Bio music label, F Communications and the mighty Transmat, to name but three, while his punishing dj and live schedule sees him play his music across the world every weekend. ‘Sublimina’ is his third full-length album – and features 15 tracks of deep and soulful machine music, from shimmering dancefloor tracks to more reflective grooves, all laced with vince’s distinctive melancholy harmony and intricate rhythm.

On this third Delsin Invites.. night we will see Vince Watson bringing a special live performance, all in style of this mighty album. Headspace label boss Tom Churchill will join him along the night with the best blend of real techno around.

Delsin Records is bringing leftfield soulful techno since 1996, this year up to it’s 50th release, and the rich discography includes debut albums by artists such as Aardvarck, 154, Dynarec, Future Beat Alliance and others. The Amsterdam’s technolabel has been throwing irregular parties since 2001. Previous editions included performances by Dynarec, Optic Nerve/Keith Tucker, Lucky&Easy, Ultradyne, Deepart, Strand, Domu, Future Beat Alliance, Dribs, Shake a.o. New release coming up: Planet Delsin, compilation with all new unreleased tracks, CD & 2xLP!
