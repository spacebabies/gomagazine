Th'acquisition

Thacquisitions love for playing music started as a hobby when he played his music just for fun, entertainment and mostly at friends parties. Speelt Friday 09 January op Illynoiz.

What started out as a casual activity grew into a serious hobby and at the end a fulltime profession.

He became the man that determines the atmosphere that gives you that good vibration which leaves you all sweaty and trembling with that peep in your ears at the end of the show. He is the resident DJ at the Redrum parties, Hostile take over and DWA … The first recurrent party he organized and managed by his own was Interceptor, a drum and bass party with a rough attitude and hardcore supporters. In 2002 the name was changed into Drum and Breakz but the concept stayed the same; a monthly venue of freshly squeezed dram and bass nights filled with great vibes and enthusiastic atmosphere, Thacqusitions gladly receives a spanking by his turntables along with applauses from his follow DJs, VJs, MCs and raves.
