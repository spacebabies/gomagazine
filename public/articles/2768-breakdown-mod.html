BREAKDOWN @ MOD, Hasselt // 8 oct 2004

For the third time, we invade the Muziekodroom with an astounishing line-up of drum&bass, electro, breaks, hiphop and r&b. The main hall will host the drum&bass room, with topshotter and old school legend SHY FX as main feature. If you haven’t heard ‘ORIGINAL NUTTAH’ (feat Uk Apachi), ‘SHAKE UR BODY’ or ‘MURDERATION’ before, you simply have to come down. Local heroes WONTIME and MURDOCK fill the voids, assisted by MC’s PROSKIPT (of Major League fame – NL!) and CHARJAN Vocalist (Jamaica to London to Hasselt – this bwoy nuh play; trust!)
