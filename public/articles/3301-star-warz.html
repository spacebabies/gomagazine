STAR WARZ - saturday, April 2nd 2005

Star Warz remains the leading Drum and Bass event in Belgium. This was proven once more on the last edition which was sold out again before 12 o’clock.

Star Warz always unites the biggest international names ( Goldie, Grooverider, Ed Rush, Marcus Intalex, Doc Scott, Andy C and many more) with the most talented newcomers ( Matrix, Simon Bassline Smith, Silver, Bailey, Concord Dawn, ... ) and big names and young talents from Belgium. As such Star Warz represents all styles and evolutions within the scene and gives their public a broad look into the Drum and Bass spectrum.

Star Warz, wants to keep innovating – not only as a concept but also as a musical genre. Because what makes the parties such a success is the crowd obviously: an open-minded, up-for-it public that admires Drum and Bass, wants to be surprised and that creates that special atmosphere that everybody’s raving about !

This time they have chosen A Sides from Eastside Recordings and Metalheadz, amongst others because this guy has over 250 releases to his name. He brings along MC Fats who also collaborated on his album Follow the Groove and who is one of his favorite MC’s.

Second guest is Dom who has produced for all the most prolific labels around as No-U-Turn, Moving Shadow, 31 Recordings, Prototype, ... you name them. He’s also just started his own Dom & Roland Productions, a label to definitely watch out for in the future! His favorite MC is MC Verse who also just started his own label Crunch.

And with MC Jay from Holland joining our residents, Millennium Kru, the crowd will definitely be heated up by such a strong MC-force, next to groundbreaking dj’s.
