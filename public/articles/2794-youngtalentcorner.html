Youngtalentcorner - Friday, 22nd October - Vooruit, Ghent

Young Talent Corner (YTC) is a concept by Poppunt and Kozzmozz which is entering it’s 3rd year of existence. The goal of YTC is to establish a full grown DJ-platform with playing opportunities for young, local talents on divers dance-events. This talent, which these days is not enough represented, deserves a chance and that’s why this platform wants to be the first step in their careers.
