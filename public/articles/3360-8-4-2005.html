8/4/2005: INMOTION invites The Advent live

INMOTION @ Club More officially takes off on Friday, April 8, with its two residents Steve Rachmad and Shinedoe, and a very special guest on board: The Advent live! Steve and Shinedoe would like to welcome you in the nest of their new home, where they will strive towards purity of techno sound in the private atmosphere of a small venue. This special kick-off edition aims at bringing the loyal techno public together for a revival of old-school techno memories.Â 

Due lack of regular club nights with quality music and old days-flavor in Amsterdam, INMOTION joins Morality’s urge for a new energy on the scene and aims at enriching Amsterdam with nights that would offer a steady (Detroit) techno/tech-house concept. Friday, April 8 is the kick-off party of the INMOTION monthly residency at More that falls on every second Friday of the month.

INMOTION’s residents Shinedoe and Steve Rachmad are inviting a very special guest for this edition, by many considered as one of the most influential artists of the second techno generation: The Advent live.

The Advent (real name Francisco Ferreira) enters the techno map in the late 1980s as a sound engineer for Jack Trax, engineering for Derrick May, Fingers Inc, Adonis, etc. In 1990 he co-produces several projects with CJ Bolland and works alongside the likes of Mundo Musique. In 1994 he signs the contract with Internal Records and forms The Advent along with Collin McBean. Since then The Advent have released a series of outstanding EP’s that have combined the best elements of Electro and Techno, but always generated music with a dance floor edge. In 1999 the duo parts different paths, and Cisco becomes a sole artist behind The Advent.

While he has had a long history with tons of accomplishments to hang his hat on, Cisco continues to make history. The Advent is fulfilling his vision as much as ever. He is busy touring as both a DJ and a live performer, producing for numerous labels, and running his own imprints, Kombination Research and Alpha.
