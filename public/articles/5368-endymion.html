Endymion

Endymion [Bart Revier, Bas Lint and Jelle Neys] was founded in The Netherlands in a small place called Wateringen in 1997. zaterdag 15 september op A Nightmare Outdoor.

Endymion [Bart Revier, Bas Lint and Jelle Neys] was founded in The Netherlands in a small place called Wateringen in 1997.

We made music with fast tracker and we played at small party’s and local radio stations.

In 1998 we signed a contract with Cardiac and they released a lot of tracks such as: Failure, We are the future, Mind and body as one, Demonsworld, The Core and a lot of other tracks on Supreme Intelligence. People started to call us the Darkcore Xplorers and a new sound was born.

After these releases we expanded our studio and started to work with professional equipment.

The sound of Endymion became popular which resulted in gigs on big raves all over the world like Digital Hut (Los Angeles), Mysteryland (NL), Death Expereince (Austria), Judgement Day (GER), Extreme Invasion (IT), Rave Master (Spain), Goliath (Switserland), Masters of Hardcore (NL), Megarave (NL), Thunderrave (IT), Rebound (NL), Hardcore Gladiators (GER), Defqon (NL), Electronika (France), Hardcore Resurrection (NL), Project Hardcore (Belgium) etc.

In 2000 we did our first remix for Evil activities and after that we did a lot more for artists like DJ Bike, Wedlock, Bodylotion, Nosferatu, G-shock, Meagashira, The Viper and a lot more.

In 2001 we started our own label together with Nosferatu called Enzyme records. This label is now one of the most popular labels in the hardcore scene.

By the end of july 2003 we released our second album called Catalyzed reactions.
