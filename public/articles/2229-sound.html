SOUND ARCHITECTURE AT MECCA SCHEVENINGEN

Every year we do this special intimate beach event at our favourite beachclub Mecca situated at the Noorderstrand Scheveningen. This year we intend to make this party just as nice and even better than the last editions, this will also be the last Sound Architecture event for this summer untill September. This is why we would like to invite everybody who supported us to come and relax at the beach with us good music for good people at the right location.
