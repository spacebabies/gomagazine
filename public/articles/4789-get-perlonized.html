Get Perlonized Club NL

On the 30th of September 2006 Perlon & Lofar present the 4th edition of ‘GET PERLONIZED’:

During this exciting all-nighter, Amsterdam will again be visited by well-established German producers, whose approach to house music emphasizes subtleties and space.

First up, Michael Baumann, aka Soulphiction, aka Jackmate. A live set with some heavily Chicago influenced work, making that rare crossover by taking the best from funk, soul, jazz, hip hop and house/electro, whilst still delivering something highly original where you can feel the artists soul shining through in the end result.

One of the Pantytec boys, Sammy Dee has sensual and boisterous capabilities to fill up any floor with his dj sets. Expect an oddball mix of rolling mutant house grooves.

Closing ranks is local Lofar hero Ramchez, serving up microhouse in it’s most engaging & purest form ’ esteemed electronics, non-stop 4/4, in full effect.

Our menu of performing artists:

Soulphiction [live] (Perlon) Sammy Dee (Perlon) Ramchez (Lofar, Redevice) Teyega (visuals)

Date: 30-09-2006 Time: 23.59 – 07.59 Damage: 10.- Location: Club NL – Nieuwezijds Voorburgwal 169 ’ Amsterdam

No presale ’ no guest list! Expect sounds and purification towards the essence of soul.
