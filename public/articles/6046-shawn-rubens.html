Shawn Rubens

On the 19th of January 1979, Ruben Overdulve a. k.a. Shawn Rubens was born in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. Performs Saturday 16 August on Detour en Kinq minifestival.

He developed his love for electronic music at a very young age, and started messing with his first set of turntables when he was fifteen years old. Since then he has created his own style of mixing; energetic, pounding techno beats, smooth crossover’s, combinations that almost create new records at the time of playing, his sets going out with a big bang. He also plays Drum & Bass and Hardcore…

Shawn Rubens has been playing his sets at various locations, such as the Bootleg DJ Café and the Tejas Bar in Rotterdam (where he played some 5 hour sets) and, not least, at Teqnology in the Heineken Music Hall, Amsterdam (using his aka NME ).
