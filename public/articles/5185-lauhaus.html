Lauhaus

Lauhaus is an upcoming DJ/producer in the fresh Dutch dance scene.

He started out at the late nineties mixing various styles and developed his sound into an electro-infused blend of minimal house and techno using vinyl and cd’s.

To keep surprising his audience Lauhaus always plays the hottest new releases and many unreleased tracks from himself and other style-like artists like Guido Schneider, Motio & E-contact, Troy Pierce and Ricardo Villalobos. Next to his dj-ing, Lauhaus is also the co-founder of the Traffic and Mono concepts. Traffic is a full on techno event, while the Mono concept pays attention to the more minimal side of techno and house. With the growth of the Mono and Traffic concept Lauhaus’s popularity also increased witch resulted in regular gigs al over the country like Awakenings, Voltt, Innercity, Free Your Mind festival, Planet Rose, 5 days off, Rockit festival and the FFWD Streetparade Rotterdam.

Currently he spends a lot of time in the studio which resulted in his first release that is scheduled at the end of this year. Also his studio project ‘Polder’ together with David Labeij receives a lot of positive feedback from the crowd.
