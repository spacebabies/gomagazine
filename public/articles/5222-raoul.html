‘Raoul’ Belmans, known for his DJ-residency at the FOOD club in Brussels (one of Europe’s leading house clubs) started his carreer on a local radio station at the age of 17. By the start of 1994 he played his first real deal parties and had already build himself a solid jockey reputation.

He teamed up with Dimitri in the studio two years later to explore the field of music production and soon released his first record. Nowadays his co-productions with Dimitri, under names like “Swirl People” and “Cozy Creatures” are being released on different major houselabels in the UK, USA and The Netherlands.
