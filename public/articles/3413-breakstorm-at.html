Breakstorm! at club More... 15 april

On Friday the 15th of April Breaks will finally get the attention it deserves in the Netherlands, as there will be the kick-off party of “BREAKSTORM!” at Club More. This night is a collaboration between Advanced Party and Morality.

Breakstorm! is proud to have Lee Coombs (UK) playing. Lee has built up his reputation with his uplifting break sets all around the world, but also with his releases on ‘Thrust? (his own label) and ‘Finger Lickin’. He did remixes for Paul Oakenfold, Moby, Lamb and New Order, and has made tracks with the Plump dj’s and Meat Katie, just to name a few. We’re very glad to have this talent in Club More during his world tour, presenting us the finest in UK breaks. On this night he will be supported by Rombout & Chamelian on 4 decks,DJ Mason and Egil.

The entrance is 10,- Euro. Club More will be open between 23.00 and 05.00.
