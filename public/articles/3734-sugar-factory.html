Sugar Factory presents Artfreaks

Sugar Factory is changing the program on Thursday in Sugar Factory presents Artfreaks. Artfreaks.net is a foundation for ARTists, Musicians, Performers and more who give support on several big and smaller events. From now on, everyone can send their bio & info to [email protected] to get a stage on Thursday early program.

The opening will be on Thursday the 2nd of June in Sugar Factory with Nightgallery opening of Test-Portal. Free entrance from 10 pm till Midnight. Artfreaks continu from midnight till 4 with Lupe (Unk) & Steffi (Klakson) with video by Ron Bunzl & poetry from Kain.
