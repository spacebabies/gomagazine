Groove Addicts

Groove Addicts are Jarno Cabeza and Dennis Bianci, two close friends with an all consuming passion for electronic dance music.

They were already dj-ing for several years (personal biography see below) when they decided to combine forces in a new dj-project named groove.addicts in the fall of 2004.

The two rapidly established themselves as a dj duo and within a couple of weeks they performed at some of Holland’s best parties and radio shows such as Club 11, Offcorso, Paradiso, Panama, BNN’s Weekender (3FM), Tivoli, Dance Valley 2005, Electronation radio show (KinkFM) and much more…

Groove Addicts quickly developed their own style and play an unique and eclectic mix that varies from minimal house to acid and electro, and from techhouse to breaks and techno. They have been picked up by Electronation and became one of their resident dj’s.

Groove Addicts: expect a high caliber dj-team playing quality electronic music with great passion and enthusiasm.
