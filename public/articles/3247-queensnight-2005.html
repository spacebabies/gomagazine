QUEENSNIGHT 2005: GONZO @ The Supperclub, Friday April 29, 2005 (11PM-5

After a killer sold-out New Years Eve event, featuring an insane collection of some 500 costumes, GONZO returns on Queensnight 2005. We will be partying on this special night in the main supperclub (Jonge Roelensteeg 21). Even more special because the supperclub has only just restyled Le Salon Coloré into Le Chambre Obscure… the supperclub Darkroom! This new area was opened on February 26 with a celebrity fuelled launch and continues to attract attention. It’s not everyday that supperclub create new spaces.   You really can’t miss this GONZO event. For 2005 we won’t be throwing as many parties as 2004. Only on special nights and in special circumstances….   You’ll be pleased to know that even though Le Salon Coloré is no more, the extremely luscious fantastically fun Le Bar Rouge remains. Rouge is a great intimate party room. Probably our favourite in the whole city. It was the location for some spectacular Punk Rock Karaoke during the very first party GONZO ever held in supperclub. Rest assured it will again be a crazy cool space for Queensnight 2005. More info on that later!
