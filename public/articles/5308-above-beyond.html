Above & Beyond

Above & Beyond are one of the most exciting talents in the global dance music scene today.

Their genre-defining productions – often released on their own Anjunabeats label – have become an essential tool for the world’s leading DJ’s and have set the benchmark for the new generation of trance producers. Above & Beyond – aka Jono Grant, Tony McGuinness and Paavo Siljamäki – have recorded under a number of different aliases including Aalto, Free State, Nitromethane, Oceanlab, P.O.S and Tranquility Base.

Equally comfortable behind the decks as in the studio, as DJ’s Above & Beyond have rocked dancefloors at venues such as Godskitchen, Gatecrasher and Cream, as well as internationally.

The boys recently scooped Radio 1’s prestigious Essential Mix Of The Year Award, prompting Radio 1 resident Pete Tong to describe them as ‘the UK’s new trance superstars’. Above & Beyond were also shortlisted in 3 categories at the recent Miami Winter Music Awards, including in the category of best dance group alongside the Chemical Brothers, Scissor Sisters and Basement Jazz.

Recent productions from Above & Beyond have included ‘Surrender’ and ‘Getting Away’ under their Tranquility Base guise, and the latest Above & Beyond single, ‘No One On Earth’, which featured Faithless’ Zoe Johnston on vocals. The tracks have found with favour with everyone from Radio 1’s Pete Tong and Judge Jules, to international A-listers such as Paul Van Dyk, Oakenfold and Tiesto. In addition to their own productions, the trio have performed remix duties for the likes of Madonna, Dido, Britney and Delerium.

In June 2005 Above & Beyond will release ‘Anjunabeats Volume 3’, the latest in a series of mix albums designed to showcase the best music on A&B’s Anjunabeats label.

Then in September, the trio will release their first artist album, set to feature collaborations with Carrie Skipper, Zoe Johnston and Andy Moor, known for his work with Tilt and as half of Leama & Moor. The first single from the album Above & Beyond + Andy Moor ‘Air For Life’ will be released in July.
