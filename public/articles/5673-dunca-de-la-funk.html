Dunca de la Funk

Duncan has been obsessed with music from a very young age. Speelt Thursday 24 January op Riot.

Growing up in the states influenced Duncan to spin R&B, Hip-Hop and work on his scratching skills. Moving back to the Netherlands drastically changed Duncan’s style to House/Dance and what can be seen as todays Electronic music. Like every other dj Dunca got in touch with all the different styles of dance movements and formed his own sound after a few years. An uplifting blend of electronic house music that glides along the paths of the Minimal movement to the deep twisted full spectrum house sound.

Dunca de la Funk is what we call ‘the new generation’ DJ that Holland adds to the scene. Just two turntables are not enough; working with homemade samples for cool intro’s, 2 turntables, 2 cd-players, samplers and of course the Pioneer mixers and effect units
