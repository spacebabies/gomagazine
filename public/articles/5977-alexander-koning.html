Alexander Koning

Alexander koning is the owner of TRANCENTRAL RECORDSTORE in Haarlem (Netherlands) and founder of several labels like Bassethound, Dolls, Madnurse and Badgirls . Speelt Saturday 02 August op Loveland Festival.

He is also the promoter of the welknown ‘Eye on the Future’ parties. Who where at the forfront of bringing techno to the netherlands in the early 90 ties.And still do parties on regular bases. In 1998 Alexander decided it was time to make the step into producing.

First with minimal equipment (a bedroom studio) but as the years progressed the studio grew.. Now he is the proud owner of a nice studio called “bahnstrasse”. Foreign labels also began to show interest in this guy from Haarlem and this resulted in releases and rmxes for welknown labels like : zync,bamboo,bush,concept,sway,recon warriors,bellboy & subliminal to name a few.

Alexander koning is also a welknown DJ in the dutch scene with gigs on Dancevalley(mainstage), Horizonz, 5 days off,beachbop,bevrijdingsfestival ,Houseclassics, Virtual environment ,new frontier ect ect

And steady appearances in welknown clubs and nights like lichtfabriek, Stalker, Patronaat,mazzo and melkweg , you can say he is a indemand and loved DJ. From 1999 he expanded his horizon and started to play throughout the world on regular bases. This Brought him to all the corners of the world and made him a loved dj throughout the globe.

Alexander plays and produces a mixture of musical styles. But the base will always be techno, or as he says: ‘my style of playing varies from house to techno, but I’m not that much into the harder styles of music, for me a good night features a mixture of several styles so you can build up a night to a climax alone or with others’

Short discography : Releases on : madnurse,bassethound,bush,zync,badgirls,babe,bamboo,reconwarriors,Bellboy,concept,sway and more Worked and made rmxes for : mateo murphy,valentino kanzyani,leandro gamez,kevin saunderson,hertz,joel mull,sebastien leger,vitamin d ect ect.
