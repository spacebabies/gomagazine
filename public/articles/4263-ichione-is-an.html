IChiOne is an initiative celebrating the innovative & eclective

Thus IChiOne wants to serve the personal welfare of individuals by providing auditive delights, pleasurable beats and different art forms.

I, stands for mind Chi, stands for subtle energy One, the interdependence of both They are One.

Come, join us and Celebrate 2005 with our last drum and bass party of the year.

Next to our Residents-Big Eye, John Doe of Counter Intelligence, Pressure of Redzone and our host Quest One of Amsterdamaged will perform

Martsman , an upcoming producer with emphasis on producer, this prolific song maker has Releases on Exegene Records, Commercial Suicide and forthcoming releases on the American based label-Offshore Recordings.

With affection for prominent basslines and stumbling breaks in combination with glitchy edits and bleepy minimalism Martsman has developed a unique musical vocabulary which extends and transcends the idea of drum’n’bass with special focus on all kinds of Electronica.

Spirit is a legend alive in the world of drum and bass from the early days of Rave to the reign of Timeless Recordings when d&b split off to become a path of music in its own right. With releases on Renegade Hardware, Timeless, CIA, Phantom Audio, Metalheadz and his own progressive imprint InnerActive this giant continues to speak in a non vocal but poignant melodic voice

With passion for techno-based aesthetics and monstrous bottom end basses complemented with tough beats and subtle trance-inducing evolutions of complex sound scapes, Spirit, describes his musical journey as “Atmospherics with a groove”

There is a hidden world of good music that’s being widely ignored, Music that’s there to affect the mind as well as the legs This event’s goal is to broaden the spectrum and perception of drum and bass. Welcome in our realm
