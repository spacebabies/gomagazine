365MAG AROUND THE WORLD @ Club SOL PAUL SPARKES, MUZIKJUNKI, ADAM YEHIE

After three highly successful editions of 365Mag at Club Sol, this next installment will be another night of musical quality from the 365mag crew. On 18th March 365mag Around the World touches down in Holland once again at Club SOL on the Rembrandtsplein in Amsterdam.

This month sees rising star Muzikjunki guesting on the turntables. The Dutchman enjoys a welcome return after playing a stunning breaks set, including many of his own productions, on his debut at the club in January. Muzikjunki also turns in high-quality product for release on the Czech Republic’s F2 label. He also runs his own nights in Rotterdam and Dordrecht.

Mainstay resident DJ Paul Sparkes (UK) will again take you on a musical journey into the unknown just the way we like it, with a sound that is difficult to define, but will no doubt encapsulate the best in spaced-out tribal, warm tech-house and more. 

Up-and-comer Adam Yehiel won the TWSTD talent competition recently, and it’s his warm, minimal and funky style that sees him earn his first appearance at a 365mag event. With a guest slot on Radio 3FM this weekend, expect to hear more from this DJ in the near future. Expect a solid warm-up from the man from the Med! 

DJ Café Sol is renowned for it’s intimate atmosphere and it’s excellent sound, light and décor. It’s commitment to quality music and an exceptional vibe make the venue the logical choice. It’s location in the heart of Amsterdam attracts a cosmopolitan mix of clubbers.  Therefore, Sol is the perfect place to enjoy ground-breaking new music.

So, join us at 365mag’s Around The World party Friday 25th January at DJ Cafe Sol, Amsterdam.
