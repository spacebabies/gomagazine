Born in 1979 this artist / promoter has lived in places ranging from Belgium to the States, but finally settled in Amsterdam, where he started mixing in 1999, and promoting in 2000. Two projects are still running: MONO and Traffic.

As a resident for both, Aron gets to give the crowd its monthly dose of warm and funky techno. He is currently studying history at the University of Amsterdam, and also flirting with musical production. Once a month he writes a column for Beatfreax.nl (see homepage). Future plans include a platinum album, a New York Times bestseller, and finding the girl that will share his life as a lamafarmer.
