365MAG.COM presents CHICKS ON SPEED

Now for something totally different! After the success of the last 365Mag event at Panama with Tilt’s Explorer album launch, there is a change in direction for the 365mag crew this month. On 25th March 365mag is proud to present indie punk electro rockers Chicks On Speed!

Expect Panama’s main room to be stormed by Berlin-based Melissa Logan, Alex Murray-Leslie and Kiki Moorse, better known as Chicks On Speed! With a sound that can be defined as Electropunk, the three girls will bring their unique form of musical anarchy to the club. The trio found fame with the track Kaltes Klares Wasser (Cold Clear Water) in 2001 and since then have gone on to become internationally renowned, touring the US and all over Europe many times. The act have delivered many tracks on their own label Chicks On Speed Records and released their latest album entitled Push The Spacebar last month. It promises to be a riotous show as only they know how to deliver!

Natalie Data will bring her unique solid house and electro sound to the same room. Exchanging her native Russia for the UK a few years back, she now resides in the UK and hosts the forward-thinking Qaraj Show on Ministry of Sound Radio. The main-room will be expertly warmed up for the trio by the ever-capable resident, Paul Sparkes (UK).

The second room sees Panama residents Jan Boom and Jonas Braun (Panama residents) playing all things deep and varied, keeping it high quality as always.
