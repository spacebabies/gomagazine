Terry Toner

DJ en producer Terry Toner komt rechtstreeks uit de Nederlandse underground house scene. Speelt Saturday 29 December op Rex… Electronation live.

DJ en producer Terry Toner komt rechtstreeks uit de Nederlandse underground house scene. Inmiddels heeft  Roy Avni (Electronation) zich ontfermd over dit talent en heeft het publiek hem ook ontdekt. Ondanks zijn jonge leeftijd heeft al veel bereikt qua produceren en draaien. Deze zomer tourt Terry Toner in het buitenland, gelukkig komt hij wel even terug voor Molotov en Mysteryland.

English:

Born in 1982 this Dj/Producer can be considered as ‘the new kid on the electronic block’. He grew up with the music his parents played for him, the Beatles, Buddy Holly and Bill Hailey. How this influenced his music style today, he quote’s: “I’ve got absolutely no idea”. He first came in contact with electronic music at the age of ten, after being forced by his older nephew to listen to his rave records, he was instantly hooked and there was no way back.

His musical interests do not limit themselves to techno/house and old-skool electro/disco but he also has a unknown weakness for the campy poptunes that Stock, Aitken and Waterman produced in the eighties. This mixture of musical interests and his banging style of mixing gives him his specific ‘Terry Toner’ sound. Nowadays he mainly divides his time between Dj-ing, producing and his study. His plans for the future are simple: Conquer the world!!
