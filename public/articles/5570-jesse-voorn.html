Jesse Voorn

Jesse was born in 1975 in the south of Holland and was injected with the music virus from his early childhood. Speelt Saturday 24 November op A Night With....

Jesse was born in 1975 in the south of Holland and was injected with the music virus from his early childhood. Both his parents were classically trained musicians, and he followed up on that by taking piano lessons. As a teenager however, he picked up on hiphop and rock music in the late 80’s and went on for a different kind of music. That was until he discovered dance music in the early 90’s. He started to party and lost himself in the club scene. But just dancing wasn’t enough: he wanted to make the people dance. That’s why he bought his first records, from early hiphop to old school acid, techno and drum ‘n bass and started spinning, mainly on small parties from friends. In 1994, just for the fun of it, he produced a couple of house tracks with two friends, which were received well but never released.

As a DJ Jesse Voorn had his first residency in Hedon (Zwolle), where he experimented a lot with different styles of music and also developed a broad taste in music that was warmly welcomed by the audience. Because he really hates it to limit himself to one particular style of music, he grabs pieces of different kinds of music that influenced him during the years. Why play just one genre if there’s so much out there? Tired of the monotone four-to-the-floor dance music, he came up with a unique blend of styles and created a brand new and energetic sound with an eclectic mixture of electro, house, rock, breakbeats and pop that truly rocks the dancefloor gets the crowd moving: He calls it simply electronicpoprockdance. Besides that, Jesse likes to put a bit of funk to his slamming and trashy housebeats.

Jesse is a real crowd pleaser and definitely spins for his audience. But he also likes to give them something to remember, and he achieves this with an excellent choice of records, combining both well known as less known tunes in his eclectic mixing style. Jesse already played a lot of renowned clubs and dance events like Amsterdam’s Hotel Arena, Powerzone, Panama, Club Zyon, So What and Twstd, Haarlem’s Muziekfabriek and Bevrijdingsfestival 2005 and Zwolle’s Hedon and Bevrijdingsfestival 2004. He also is a much appreciated and regular guest at the Amsterdam based Motion parties and recently played a successful gig at Ministry Of Beats on 3FM national radio. His latest big performance was at Rockit Open Air.

Jesse played alongside dj’s like Don Diablo, Laidback Luke, Secret Cinema, Ken Ishii, Mylo, James Zabiela and Derrick May. When Jesse is not deejaying, he works as a music journalist for several media and even takes some steps on the producing path again.

Utrecht kleurt groen met Dr. Lektroluv

Op 27 oktober is er weer een Utrechtse consult van Dr. Lektroluv. Dit keer verplaatst hij zijn laboratorium naar de Central Studios waar hij alle bezoekers infecteert met zijn geheime, groene virus. Dit virus veroorzaakt opwinding en vervoering, spanning en sensatie maar bovenal een euforische ontlading. En met een beetje mazzel, een verkleuring.
