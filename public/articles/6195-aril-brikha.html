Aril Brikha

Aril Brikha is natuurlijk bekend van zijn dansvloerkraker ‘Groove La Chord’ uit 1998 en zijn geprezen debuutalbum ‘Deeparture In Time’ uit 2001. Speelt Saturday 22 November op TWSTd invites Aril Brikha.

Deze zomer kwam zijn nieuwe album ‘Ex Machina’ uit. Een plaat vol zweverige synths, prachtig gedrapeerd over dikke baslijnen. Ook maakt Brikha met zijn live-set furore. Eerder dit jaar speelde hij het Free Your Mind festival plat met een diepe, mooie technoset.

English:

Aril Brikha is a 25 year old producer from Stockholm who initially discovered electronic music listening to the likes of Depeche Mode, Front 242 and Jean Michel Jarre before discovering the more futuristic sounds of people like Robert Hood and Underground Resistance.

When not getting any respond in the native country he sent a demo to some of his favourite record labels stateside. Before he knew it Derrick May released The Art Of Vengeance EP featuring the track Groove La Chord on his Fragile record label and was playing it all around the world. This was followed by the release of the Deeparture In Time album also on Transmat. His livesets has been highly appreciated all the way from DEMF in Detroit to The Fabric in London, playing his take on Detroit-techno with his special soul and funk to it.

2005 saw the appearance of the long awaited EP “Prey for Peace” on Music Man. The upcoming album will collect a heap of unreleased new material which clubbers have been enjoying already at recent performances.
