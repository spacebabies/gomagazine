Diesel-U-Music Festival

Saturday May 1st Diesel-U-Music will hit Ghent in storming fashion in the old Eskimo Factory situated on Wiedauwkaai . Our judge and jury have listened to hundreds of demos in the past few weeks and the best young talents have tried to prove themselves in Culture Club last March 10th. Careful deliberation will see the best new talent in every genre play alongside the big guys from the national and international music scene on May 1st.
