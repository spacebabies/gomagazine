Prog As A Frog - Chapter 2 - February 5, 2006 - Amsterdam

After processing the initial progression of paragraphs in psychedelism, the book re:opens for all seeing eyes to browse through Chapter Two. Join us in the Royal Warehouse as our sonic adventure continues up and down the blacklit path, following ancient trails and future trends, freaking in and spacing out, as we dance in tribal trance.

  • Lined up for your Aural Pleasure are: - CD presentation: Servellen (www.cytopia.org)

- Deejays: Funky Dragon (Synergetic rec / Austria) Bart (Sonic Dragon / Empathy) Dogo (9LoH / Prog as a Frog)

  • Chosen to perform Optical Intrusions: - visuals by Rorschac h & Rorschach - decoration by D-Core
  • Besides all of the above, we have a couple of bonus items in store behind door number 3: - Music production workshop: introduction to Cubase by Zebra-N - Culinary intermezzo by Pow Pow, on from 17:30 - History of flyerart exposition
  • Furthermore: - the first 30 visitors to arrive, will receive a free promo-CD (courtesy of Doof rec & Sonic Dragon)! - fullfill the CrossFnord and compose the secret verse, to win a Special CD (courtesy of MoonDrop): > 5 winners will be picked from the magic hat and announced at 6ix o’clock (for Crossfnord, see below).
  • Stardate: SunDay February 5, 2005
  • Missiontime: 15.00 – 01.00
  • Location: Wilhelminapakhuis
  • Address: Veemkade 576, Amsterdam
  • Entrance: 5neuro
