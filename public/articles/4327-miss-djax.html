Miss Djax European Tour 2006

Over the next few months, notorious Miss Djax will be crossing borders as her European Tour is about to kick off! Her debut album RAW is a huge hit. Now all techno, acid and general dance lovers can finally check out Miss Djax performing her favourite RAW tracks LIVE!

Miss Djax’s first live performance was at Q-Base 2005 and it was an instant hit! Since then Miss Djax is booked as a live act 9 out of 10 times!

On stage Miss Djax will go to the max with the Eyesupply vj’s. They mix up the beautiful Alan Oldham, Djax graphics with the most extreme horror images on immense screens. A true tech treat!

Background information

Miss Djax: “RAW is created from the heart; unpolished, rough and with a fat wink to my big love – Old-Skool Techno”.

After working hard as a dj for over fifteen years, Miss Djax finally found time to spend time in the studio to record her first ever artist album. The result is twelve pumpin’ oldskool techno tracks.

With RAW she reverts to the techno sound of the early nineties. ‘It is a tribute to an old love that doesn’t die’, thus Miss Djax. The so-called Djax-feeling prevails on the album. ‘That means that I do things on my gut feeling, by following my heart. For me there is no need to analyze the music I make. This will only result in more artificiality. I hope that this record carries the Djax feeling. But most of all RAW has become a record to party with!’

On RAW, Miss Djax worked with DJ Rush. He too, cherishes is own unique style. DJ Rush did his vocal magic on tracks as ‘Club Hammer’ and Sick of U’. Miss Djax: ‘His voice fits these tracks perfectly. No wonder that those are my favorites. My other favorites are ‘Brain Candy’ which is a mean acid trip and ‘Techniek Stad’. To me this tracks reflects Eindhoven
