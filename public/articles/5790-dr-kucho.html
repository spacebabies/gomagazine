Dr Kucho

Since 1994 Dr. Speelt Saturday 22 March op Framebusters.

Kucho! has brought out over 140 tracks on a large number of labels (Africanism, Virgin, Sony, Defected …), both remixes and new work. Many of these have reached number one in thousands of charts worldwide, such as “Patricia never leaves the house” and “La luna”, for Weekend Records (1998-2002), making an essential contribution to the surge of Spanish house music, which burst onto the international stage from nowhere. Or others from his new phase in 2003 after bringing out his own record label – “Belmondo Rulez 2.0”, “Lies to yourself” ’ and of course his latest creation with Gregor Salto, “Can’t stop playing”, which held the number one slot on Dutch radio charts for 5 weeks.

At Disc Doctor Records he only produces tracks which have the quality he demands, and all of these feature his own remixes, providing the special touch dubbed by many as “Dr. Kucho’s style”. This is precisely one of the main reasons why he is sought after by clubs around the world, a style which also makes its mark on his sessions.

Fully aware of the importance of technological progress, he ensures that his websites www.drkucho.com and www.discdoctor.es are constantly updated to allow anyone to hear his latest releases, see the flyers for the parties where he has dee-jayed, provide information on future parties, and further details on this international artist.
