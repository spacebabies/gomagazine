KLAKSON NEW RELEASE & 5-JARIG BESTAAN

Mr Cisco aka Francisco, part of Raiders Of The Lost ARP and Jolly Music, already made a huge emerge onto the scene with his releases on Pigna and Nature. This time he appears on Klakson with a nice 3 track release. Again thick, phat Italo flavoured disco, as they do best in Italy! Hot hot dancefloor stuff!

Naast dit bestaat Klakson dit jaar 5 jaar en viert dat dus op 25 juni in het Wilhelmina Pakhuis Amsterdam

Klakson 11

Mr. Cisco – Life is life EP
