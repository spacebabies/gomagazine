Zaterdag 11 februari: Oldschool Gangsters - Off Corso, Rotterdam

Oldschool Gangsters (OG’s) first party was in july 2005 and the reaction for this ‘new’ concept was good so the journey will continue!!!

This episode will bring us all to the floor where it will start and end because the sound of the future will survive and bring us all back on our feet. The memories sometimes will fade but the feeling stays and will make place for new definitions of your innermind….so be aware! Is a memory better then a picture? For a lot of people it’s something visual but never had that intensive awareness if you’re openminded to live the sound of the OG’s. The residents will make this journey approved by the crowds…so join us and be a member of them…!!!

Date: 11 februari 2006 Location: Off Corso, Rotterdam

Area1: OLDSCHOOL GANGSTERS (MAIN) Stanton (Entered The OG’s…) Miss Monica (Kick Some Beatz) DJ Rob (It Started in Detroit..+ his 909) Ricky Da Dragon (Back on Track!!!) Hosted By MC Joe (Let It Flow…)

Area2: Oldschool Gangsters (ENTRY 2 MAIN) DJ Reza (House QlassiQs ‘90-’92) Ricky Da Dragon (Chicago House Flow) Jor-in (Into Hip House and more)
