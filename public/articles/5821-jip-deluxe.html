Jip Deluxe

De diepgewortelde passie voor muziek, het verkennen van grenzen, creatief mixen en de energie van elke gig: dat is waar Jip Deluxe het voor doet. Speelt Sunday 30 March op Villa Achterdeur.

Sexy, funky house music is de specialiteit waarmee hij de dansvloeren zo ongeveer alle van toonaangevende clubs in den landen heeft beroerd, maar ook in Miami, Antwerpen en op Ibiza kennen ze zijn naam. Nu is Mystery Land aan de beurt!

www.jipdeluxe.com

English:

Like most other DJ’s, Jip Deluxe has been obsessed with music from a young age. His brother, who’s a successful musician, has been an important source of inspiration for Jip Deluxe.

The thought of creating his own music and playing it in front of large crowds eventually triggered Jip Deluxe to become a DJ as well as a producer.

It all started back in 1993, when Jip Deluxe visited one of Holland’s most prestigious clubs, Nighttown in Rotterdam, for the first time. Ronald Molendijk has been and important source of motivation for him. Jip Deluxe decides to start buying records and to improve his DJ skills. From that point on, Jip Deluxe’s musical career got on a roller coaster.

He starts with guest slots at local clubs and in a wink of an eye, he has made a name for himself among the local crowd. In 1995, Jip Deluxe got his big break though. He was offered the opportunity to play at Nighttown, alongside Ronald Molendijk. He managed to control his nerves, and made his first step towards becoming on of Rotterdam’s most popular DJ’s. A monthly residency at the largest club in his hometown followed soon after.

In 1997, Jip decides to move to Rotterdam to really follow up on his dreams. He starts out as a shop assistant at the recordstore Basic Beat in downtown Rotterdam, which is actually the epic centre of DJ talent in Rotterdam; other DJ’s that have worked at Basic Beat are Tijs Verwest ’ better known as Tiësto ’ and one of Holland’s fastest rising star at the moment, Benny Rodrigues. During the summer of 1998, Jip travelled to Greece where he joined the second edition of the Kriti Vibes, playing in front of thousands of clubbers. Furthermore, he has also played at the glamorous MTC and Super Bimbo events, which were hosted by Ted Langenbach.

Since 1998, Jip Deluxe has rocked almost every credible club in Rotterdam and he is considered to be one of the most versatile DJ’s around. He operates around funky and groovy housemusic, adapting his sound to his audience. Every time he rocks a party, he teases the crowd and turns them into a frenzy. His choice of records can be characterised by garage, nu-disco, deep, tribal and tech-house influences. In other words, this kid has got a style of his own.

Nowadays, Jip holds a residency at Now and Wow (three times voted best club in Holland), Metz/Prinsestheater and GSP/Nighttown all in Rotterdam, and he regularly plays at some of the largest events throughout the Netherlands.

In addition, he regularly showcases his talents at prestigious clubs such Now & wow, supermarket (Zurich/Switzerland), Café d’anvers (Antwerpen/ Belgium) Club EVE at Cubic, Matrixx, Stalker, Nightlive, Club More, Sinners in Heaven, Vasquesz, and of course Nighttown. Moreover, he has also played at the Amsterdam Dance Event Festival 2001 and Europe’s largest indoor dance festival Innercity at the Rai. Over the years, he has rocked some of the finest crowds around, alongside the likes of DJ Sneak, Joe T. Vanelli, D’Julz, Superfunk, Roog, and Marcello, just to name a few. Besides his gigs at clubs and parties, Jip Deluxe has also made regular guest appearance at Radio 3FM’s leading ‘Dier Op Drie’ dance show, where many superstar DJ’s made guest appearances over the past few years. Now, in 2003, Jip has entered the studios to make his own album. His first record is about to be released and will most likely cause a stir in the Dutch club circuit.
