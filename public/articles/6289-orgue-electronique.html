Orgue Electronique

Voor zover je tot de elite van een anarchie kunt horen is deze Hagenaar een van de meest vooraanstaande leden van de Clone dynastie. Speelt Tuesday 10 February op 10 Years Bootleg.

Solo en samen met Legowelt produceerde hij zeer uiteenlopende en enthousiast ontvangen 12 inches op Bunker Records en Crème Organization die niet in een muzikaal hokje zijn te vangen. De sleutelwoorden zijn analoog en Chicago, de rest is aan de dansvloer.

English:

Has anybody forgotten that in the beginning there was ‘Jack’? Orgue Electronique aka Brian Chinetti certainly has not. Linked to the worldwide renowned the Hague electro-scene and his good friend Legowelt, he has come to build-up quite a reputation over the years.

With his musical baggage he has traveled all over the world, including two Japanese tours, an Australian and several USA tours, and is known to jack you till you scream. Whilst his first records are more on a minimal electro-trip, and the tracks he did on various compilations are defined as sleazy electronic disco, his later work has above all a very thoughtful and soulful Chicago house feel.

However, Orgue Electronique is not out there to recreate a Forgotten Sound, he rather picks bits and pieces out of dance music’s rich heritage and applies them to his own new subtle yet brutal analogue techniques and rock your body all night long.
