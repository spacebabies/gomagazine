Marc MacRowland

Marc MacRowland started spinning records at the age of 16 and has now been spinning for 8 years. Speelt Friday 21 September op Marc MacRowland's BDay Bash.

Marc MacRowland started spinning records at the age of 16 and has now been spinning for 8 years. Marc experienced with different styles, but he never stopped buying housemusic. The last 4 years Marc is spinning only housemusic. Nowadays Marc is a dj / producer and party-organiser.

During a dj-contest in 2001 at Nighttown Marc was selected by a jury, who consists of Ferry Corsten and Michel de Hey, to play on the Fast Forward Dance Parade. This was a great experience. Soon he had his first residency at a club called Ministry of Dance.
