SUNYATA 11 JUNE AFTERPARTY

This Friday 11 june another Psytrance organisation called “sunyata” makes a party in the waterfront in Rotterdam, we will back them up with a outdoor afterparty on a really nice nature spot in Rotterdam.   Free outdoor  psy afterparty   Beautifull location near the river Maas, with near-by small beach, nature area and view over sky line from Rotterdam. Drinks, chaishop & small soundsystem providing progressive & full-on vibes.   Starts: approx. 06.00-.   Route:   -take Maasboulevard direction Brienenoord bridge. -After approx. 700m traffic lights: go right direction “Esch” -After approx. 400m go left, you’ll enter “nesserdijk” with on your right hand river Maas.  follow this dijk direction Brienenoord bridge -Party-area will be on your left hand, just before you go underneath Brienenoord bridge.   - by feet: walk with great views; time 20 min. - by car: 10 min.   DJ’s: FluofreaX E.T. Dr. Satan Silcon Avatar and more… chaishop by jeroen and angelique
