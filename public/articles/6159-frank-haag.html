Frank Haag

Driven by musical curiousity Haag started to collect techno records and he began to create his own sounds. Speelt Friday 14 November op 2 Years Anniversary.

After a short while first few gigs were a fact. Later on Frank played next to artists like Onur Özur, Mathias Kaden, Dan Berkson and James What, Mark August, Smirk and 2000 and One. He is now known as one of the few forward thinking techno producers in Amsterdam that challenges the minimal fad with a deeper sense of understanding. His performances comprise of a combination of edgy abstract minimalism and menacing rhythmic funk with deep travelling sounds which give creed to his motto "The dance floor is for the feet as well as the mind". Artists that give full support: Mark Henning, Someone Else, Ahmet Sisman, Pherox, Andre Crom, Sandrien and Flowing. Frank has released on Dualblock recordings and Pinksilver Digital and more releases are coming up on Thema records (NYC) and Slash // , a new label by Ahmet Sisman (Stock 5) and Tim Querengaesser AKA Pherox (Catenaccio records).
