X-Rated Live in the Sugarfactory on June 18

On Saturday the 18th of june, it is time for Electronation?s X-Rated in the Sugar Factory. This time, its live, its Dutch and its full of energy! The party will be made by 5 Dutch bands that create a whole new sound in the electronic music scene. Together with Electronation resident DJ Herr Arter, the live music is brought to you by Dexter (Klakson, Clone), Comtron (Blacklabel), Rude 66 (Bunker, Viewlexxx), Alden Tyrell (Clone, Viewlexx) and De Jeugd van Tegenwoordig (Magnetron Music, Top notch, Pias).   The special element for this party is of course the live element from 5 different acts from a few of Holland´s most inspiring labels, all with a distinctive style and all producers of some really great music. Dexter is an artist with already a vast amount of releases on his account and we?re still counting, with his newest 12? coming out soon. Expect an nice blend of funky electro and raw beats. Alden Tyrell, also part of the Clone crew, has his early influences from old hip hop and the electro music, and has made some classic remixes like The IF track ?space invaders are smoking grass?. Rude 66 has been making electronic music ever since 1986, being one of the pioneers in the ´The Hague acid sound´, later mixing these influences with uptempo and rocking electro. Comtron?s music can be described as a serious boost of energetic electro-funk. Having produced the hit ‘You’ve Got My Love’ from the on Hed Kandi released album ´Ready´ as one of his many alter egos Bastian, Comtron will certainly show their diversity in music during their performance on the night. Last but not least, De Jeugd van Tegenwoordig. They have recently boosted the hitlists with their new hiphop electronic track ?Watskeburt? and will certainly pump the show with their live performance that can be seen a the clash between hiphop and electro. During the night, the visuals will be brought by the visual collective 310k. The party will have its presale at the AUB and at Boudisque. The Presale price is 10 E and tickets at the door are 15 E.  Doors open at 23.00 Sugar Factory, Amsterdam, Lijnbaansgracht 238. For more information, please check www.electronation.nl or www.sugarfactory.nl.
