Sandeep

Als één van de drijvende krachten achter Love2party en als dj loopt hij al een flinke tijd mee. Speelt Sunday 11 January op Dance Erger Je Niet.

Influenced by groovy Rotterdam tech-house, US westcoast deephouse and raw Chicago house, Love2party.nl front man Sandeep started spinning in 2001. With monthly nights in Las Palmas, Off Corso, Nighttown and Winkel van Sinkel his dj star rose and soon he was asked to play all around the Netherlands. Since then he played with the likes of Laurent Garnier, Derrick May, Basement Jaxx, Kevin Saunderson and Mr C and played big parties like Free Your Mind Festival, Rockit Open Air, Solar Weekend, Just Jamm Outdoor, Cocoon Clubbing, Global Kitchen, Lunapark and Extrema Exclusive to name a few.

The summer of 2005 was the real nation-wide breakthrough for Sandeep. He played several big festivals like Free Your Mind in Arnhem, mainstage on Just Jamm in Naaldwijk and Extrema’s Solar Weekend and a very well received pumping set on Rockit Open Air. To top things off, Sandeep has been nominated as best new talent for the “gouden kabouter” awards.

Nowadays, next to playing many parties and clubnights, Sandeep holds residencies in the Winkel van Sinkel in Utrecht, the Thalia Lounge in Rotterdam and has recently added the Escape in Amsterdam to his list. Off course he is also the resident for all Love2party events.

His style can be described as a suprising and distinct mix between (deep)house, minimal, groove and tech-house. Deep, groovy and uplifting with an energetic drive.
