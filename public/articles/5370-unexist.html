Unexist

Real Name: F. Iapicca. Alias for DJ Jappo. Hardcore/Gabber DJ & Producer. Staat zaterdag 15 september op A Nightmare Outdoor.

Real Name: F. Iapicca Profile: Alias for DJ Jappo. Hardcore/Gabber DJ & Producer

Unexist breaths and lives hardcore. ‘Unleash the Brutality’ he calls it in his own words. ‘Hardcore makes me loose my anger, pain and agression. It’s an addiction that’ll never go away.’

Francesco is always busy developing his characteristic Unexist-sound. Under his alias Dj Jappo, he scored a big hit with EXLXAXL. He made Industrial Strength Records a succesfull company. He produced and played together with dj phenomenons like Lenny Dee, Oliver Chessler, Promo and Manu le Malin. In the near future his record label Fuck this World will start at ProArtistmanagement. While living in New York for a year, Unexist immersed himself in the scratch-scène. By now, the Italian turntable virtuoso is the best scratch-master we know in hardcore.

Unexist started his dj carreer in the famous Club #1. He performed in Evolution (Swiss), CBGB’s (New York), played at Multigroove, Hardcore Ressurection and Thunderdome. He also performed together with Dj Starscream, aka Dj # (0) from Slipknot, the most succesfull metal band at this moment.
