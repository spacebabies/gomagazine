Optronica festival, July 20-24 NFT, London

Addictive TV playing live on the roof of the National Theatre, Skoltz_Kolgen, Masakatsu Takagi, People Like Us, The Sancho Plan at the NFT and DJs Joakim, Si Begg, Legowelt and a whole pool of international VJs at the Spitz in East London. Optronica takes place on July 20-24, 2005 at the NFT, the bfi London IMAX cinema – the biggest screen in Britain – and other venues and features live audiovisual performances, cinema screenings, installations and talks.  Organised by AV artists and DVD label Addictive TV and curators Cinefeel in collaboration with the National Film Theatre (NFT).  Supported by the Arts Council of England.
