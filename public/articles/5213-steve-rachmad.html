Steve Rachmad mixt DJ Mag

Steve Rachmad mixes a CD for the June edition of UK's DJ Mag. The mix contains tracks of Dutch artists only, and is recorded live at Sugar Factory in Amsterdam, as part of the Dutch project Satellite 31.

Among tracks by artists like Aardvarck, Michel de Hey & Grooveyard, Darko Esser & Benny Rodrigues, Melon and Rejected, two of Rachmad's own tracks from his recent album Neo Classica on Sino also appear on the CD.
