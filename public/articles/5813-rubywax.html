Rubywax

Rubywax is de resident en uitvinder van GLDJS en de bijbehorende GLDJS draaistijl. Speelt Friday 21 March op Girls Love DJs.

Bij Girls Love DJ’s draait Rubywax zeer gewaagde en experimentele floorfillers; een mix van Nederlandse folklore, house beats, rauwe hiphop en hardcore.... Zijn muzikale smaak gaat van Amsterdam naar Tokyo en van Kudelstaart naar Appingedam...Kortom zijn stijl is grenzeloos. Ongegeneerd draait hij alles wat los en vast zit ...En ja, zo hoort het ook! Naast het GLDJS project is Rubywax full time house- en electro dj van Apekooi.

English:

Dj Rubywax (Reuben Alexander, 24) started playing music in public places in 1994. He was part of a drive-in show and mainly played on school parties, student parties and a lot of weddings. Since 1996, when he bought his own imitation Technics, he started buying vinyl. Throughout the years he changed his musical styles many times, which provided him a very diverse record collection. For the last three years he is ‘officially’ known as dj Rubywax and plays mainly funky, groovy, sexy, ‘oh, doesn’t that sound familiar!’ house in a lot of different places. He is resident dj of Mme Jeanette (every Saturday), spent a lot of time at the beach this year playing at Sundale parties at Bloomingdale and is a regularly seen guest-dj at club More and the Supperclub. Besides that he is a monthly resident (together with Missing Links) at wickedJazzsounds. He particularly loves these so called ‘Missing At Wax’-sessions because you never know what to expect. Playing back2back and experimenting with live musicians brings out the most jazzy/groovy side of Rubywax. These sets can be best described as: Three hands on a mixer, creative mixing and always full of energy. Curious? Come and see for yourself! Every first Sunday of the month…
