English:

If DJ’s have, in essence become the post-modern conductor, then Marcel Bakker aka dj Marcello is the embodiment of this incarnation. A role which he seems to relish, his ability to raise the spirits of the crowd is an uncanny manifestation, something to be witnessed and to behold. Audiences delight in his role as musical genius, technician, and master of ceremony. Whether in a small provincial club or a massive rave in a metropolis, Marcello?s open approach to music, makes him a catalyst within the world wide music & dance scene.

At the age of 10, he began to become interested in music. Later, he would combine school and his economic studies with Dj-ing during the weekends at numerous clubs in the Netherlands. For twenty years he has mastered his technique and become a supreme presence within the dance scene. His power when he?s mixing is awesome and his sincere dedication to music is unwavering and refreshing.

In 1990 when he began his legendary Sunday residency at the IT club in Amsterdam, he transformed it from a top 40’s oriented disco to one of the Netherlands’ premier House/Rave gay clubs. Soon people from all over the Netherlands (and indeed from around the world)- gay and straight, the curious and the critical- would queue up to visit. It was here that he started mixing and compiling the Netherlands? first and hugely successful club related CD series, the IT cd, which had an enormous impact on the compilation CD in the Netherlands. The IT CD was something to mimic if not imitate; it became the reference.

Alongside his residency at the IT, Marcel (his nickname for those who know him best) put a little, known, small town club called Waakzaamheid on the international dance agenda. This resulted in Marcello beginning his own club called Chemistry , together with former Waakzaamheid creative force Eelko Anceaux, every Saturday night at Amsterdam’s Escape venue located on Rembrandtplein.

On Thursday evenings Chemistry presents RUSH, an acronym for Raw Urban Sexy House with resident DJ’s, Mar y Sol and Brian S. Additionally, Chemistry brings to the public forum a series of cd?s and Chemistry on Tour, a platform for all the DJ?s who have had or do have residency at Chemistry. Coming to a city near you.

Marcello has been nominated for best Dutch DJ by the Dutch DMC branch three times. He’s won this public recognition award twice. Chemistry has been chosen ‘best Dutch club night’ five times in a row.

After producing various 12 inches (including cooperations with Derrick May, Stacey Pullen and Eric Nouhan), Marcello has opened his own studio in spring 2001 and will be producing tracks as soon as humanly possible (which translated means very soon).

For 2003 Marcello will be starring at his own Marcello solo nights at Chemistry. This club-evenbt will be held every first Saturday of the month and will feature mr. Chemistry ALL night long!
