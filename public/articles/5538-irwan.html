Irwan

At a mere 23 years this rising star in Holland’s urban scene can already look back at more than 6 years of successful dj-ing. Speelt Thursday 15 November op `Lust.

At a mere 23 years this rising star in Holland’s urban scene can already look back at more than 6 years of successful dj-ing. Over the years DJ Irwan played at the hottest urban spots like The Challenge, Goldfinger and Ministry. And as a resident at Amsterdam’s hottest Urban Party, Timeless, he rocks the Powerzone (Amsterdam’s biggest club) every month.

This year DJ Irwan has been added to the line-up of Dance Valley, one of the world’s leading outdoor dance events (40,000 people), where other well-known DJ’s such as Tiesto & Carl Cox will also perform. The fact that DJ Irwan has been chosen among only 5 other urban dj’s, clearly shows he’s one of the elite dj’s in the Dutch urban scene.
