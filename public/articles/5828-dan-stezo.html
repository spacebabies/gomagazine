Dan Stezo

Biography Dan Stezo was first introduced to the art of MC-ing when he started “coppin’” Hiphop tapes at the tender age of 10. Speelt Friday 11 April op Spektrum XL.

His passion for lyricism grew and in the late 90’s he joined forces with local Hiphop outfit Ancient Scriptures. In the years that followed, Dan Stezo collaborated with several Reggae and Hiphop artists developing a unique but versatile style. He also gained considerable on-stage experience through performing at various major venues and festivals with Ancient Scriptures. In 2003, Dan Stezo was first asked to MC on Drum & Bass by long-time confidant Lenzman. The partnership proved successful and since then Dan Stezo and Lenzman began working together increasingly. Dan Stezo joined Lenzman at Future Research and was a well received guest when the crew toured the Czech Republic. Over the last two years Dan Stezo has boosted his output as a Drum & Bass MC, performing at many major Drum & Bass nights in The Netherlands and, as a regular, at the Project Funk, Illy Noiz and Bam nights. Recently Dan Stezo, Lenzman and Scarr decided to strengthen their commitment to the soulful side of Drum & Bass under the Asylum moniker. Asylum goes against the grain, providing a musical shelter in a scene dominated by dark and relentless Drum & Bass.

Throughout his musical explorations Dan Stezo hasn’t turned his back on Hiphop. Recent efforts are ‘Nederlandse start’; a dutch hiphop track with Aux on production, and a yet-to-be-named possecut recorded in Hong Kong with the likes of MC Yan, Ghostyle and Chef. Furthermore Dan Stezo is working together with MC/producer Djaga, Mic-nificent, CJ and others under the name Scriptonic. Latest release is ‘The Blueprint’; a musical journey through the history of Eurasians in Indonesia and The Netherlands thereafter. This resulted in a successful performance broadcasted on Dutch national television autumn 2005.
