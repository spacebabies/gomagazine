Daniela Haverbeck joins Reactor Bookings

Daniela Haverbeck came in contact with the electronic music scene in 1997 and has been interested in Techno and its variations ever since. This preference made her explore different styles of hard music. Chile’s biggest female techno export, has been playing in clubs and at festivals throughout South America and was nominated Dj Revelation in 2003 by Ballantines Go Play.

Drawn to play in Europe, the home of hard techno, she is now touring in Holland and Scotland. She is also a music producer who likes to twists knobs by herself. Be ready for her coming releases! Dark and powerful!
