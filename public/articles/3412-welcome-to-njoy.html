Welcome to NJOY - New Bar with Club atmosphere

NJOY a 160 capacity bar over 2 floors near the Leidseplein in Amsterdam.

The cocktail bar is based on the 1st floor, the beautiful and cosy décor allows for comfort and the ability to chat, the basement offers the club atmosphere, with the DJ Booth and another bar. In NJOY there an intimate, friendly atmosphere which aims to make each guest feel special, the music & sfeer will reflect this. The more maturer and more discerning party person will relish NJOY and will be warned up perfectly for the big club too if they wish to continue their night into the twilight hours.

OFFICIAL OPENINGS PARTY: FRIDAY 8th APRIL 2005, doors: 20.00 hrs
