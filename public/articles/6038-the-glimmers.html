The Glimmers

Het Belgische duo The Glimmers bestaat uit Mo Becha en David Fourqaert, al sinds de dagen van de disco, new-beat en acid vooraanstaande leden van de Belgische dance-scene. Speelt Monday 28 July op 10 days off.

Het eigen geluid van de twee van Gent heeft geleid tot een waslijst aan boekingen en releases. Hun sets gaan alle kanten op: van eigen edits van tachtigerjaren hits tot oldskool hiphop, electro, breaks, house en disco.

The Glimmer Twins. Influenced by DMC mix tapes, Grandmaster Flash, Beat Street and grafitti, David & Mo started their DJ activities in a small club in Ghent – Belgium, way back in 1985. Playing everything from Hip Hop to popular tunes from that time, the crowd was getting familiar with their various sets. Back in 1987, a legendary club called Fifty Five, hired them for saturdays, where they played lots of New Beat, New Wave and old skool Acid House. The summer of love (1988) came along, and also the Balearic vibe was finding its way to Belgium, which gave the Dj-sets something extra, and even more eklektic. Records like Soul II Soul ’s “Back To Life” where mixed into records like A Split Second ’s “Flesh”, followed by the B-52’s “Planet Claire” , Lil’ L ouis ’s “French kiss”, Neon Judgement’s “TV Treated” and Nitzer Ebb’s “Let Your Body Learn”..........

As House music was exploding in clubs all over Europe, The Glimmer Twins took the best of those releases and mixed them into their sets, without being House Dj’s, but keeping the eklektik vibe by mixing re-edits of disco tracks inbetween, and even popular tunes from the likes of Prince, Madonna and Bohannon. As people were getting bored of the non-stop house and early techno DJ’s, The Glimmer Twins started to get a reputation of being good party DJ’s, without being to trendy and without being old fashioned..

As the years passed by, The Glimmer Twins were known all over Belgium and they played in almost every club or venue in Belgium. Halfway the nineties, several promotors started parties on monthly bases, like for example the “Free The Funk” parties in Ghent, the “Kozzmozz” parties in Ghent, Brussels, and Antwerp, and off course the mother of all parties, ” ESKIMO”, were at it’s peak (in 1998) more then 7000 people came to party in five different rooms in an old underwear factory in Ghent. The music was brought with excelence by local supertalents ‘TLP aka Troubleman’, ‘2 many DJ’s’ ‘The Glimmer Twins’ and this with international high class guests..

During that period, David had a ‘regular’ job behind the counter of Belgium’s most influential record & import shop “Music Man”, where he was well placed to judge the what’s hot, what’s not.His passion for music, sound and knowledge made him quit his job and take up an audio engineering course in Amsterdam (SAE), subsequently landing a stint as A&R for the legendary R&S Records.Nowadays David is doing some A&R for Eskimo Recordings together with Mo & Dirk. During the last years their impressive record collection has proven to be the base for several succesfull compilation albums, including the stunning ‘Eskimo’ series (for Eskimo Recordings), the ‘Free The Funk’ series (for R&S Records), ‘Straight From Vinyl Session One’ (for R&S Records), and the fantastic ‘Blue Note Sidetracks Volume One’ (for Blue Note / EMI).

In addition to that they did a couple of re-edits and remixes for labels like ‘R&S Records’, ‘Wally’s Groove World’, ‘Blue Note Records’, ‘Eskimo Recordings’ and the UK based ‘Crosstown Rebels’ by Damian Lazarus… The Glimmer Twins are now resident DJ’s at Belgium’s infamous Culture Club ( www.cultureclub.be ) where they share the decks with high quality guests. Nowdays they play allover The world….Fabric London, Lux Lisboa, Pulp Paris, Cookies Berlin, Munich, Barcelona, Madrid, Warsaw, NY, Miami …To name but a few!They are even residents at the famous ‘Bugged Out’ nights in the Uk. They also did some fashion shows for D&G, Y3(Adidas), Diesel etc…..

Glimmer Twins, Mo & Benoelie Members: David Fouquaert, Mo Becha
