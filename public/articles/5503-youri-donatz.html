Youri Donatz

(Afkomstig van dj)

At the age of 14 Youri Donatz already made his own compilation tapes for family and friends. Two years later he bought two turntables and was quickly adapted to the Art of mixing. Then he started DJ-ing at birthday- and student parties and soon began to create his own style. His interest in music developed through the years, and he decided to dedicate himself entirely to Real Soulfull House music.

In October 1998 he participated in a DJ contest (1st price) and organized together with some friends parties named Sounds like.. (Keywords: live musicians and real house music!) In April 1999 DRC en Club Risk gave Youri Donatz his first big opportunity to play all over the Netherlands; Sans Souci, Danspaleis, The Challenge, Soundmuseum, Hollywood, Nightlive, Red & Blue (Antwerpen).

In the new millennium Youri Donatz is resident DJ at “Monza (Utrecht)”, “Sugar zebralounge (A'dam),Rotterdammeetsamsterdam Las Palmas (R’dam) Röntgen (Apeldoorn), “TGIF Sinnes (A'dam)" and Sounds like "Manhattan" where he spins i.e. with; Seamus Haji, Pete Heller, Bob Sinclar, Cleon Macknack, Victor Coral, DJ Roog, Stonebrigde, Marcella, Erick-E, Rashid, Quinten de Rozario & Jessie K, Cassie 6, KC the Funkaholic, Brian S, D-Rashid, Marcello, Ronald Molendijk and more. One thing led to another en Youri Donatz was also booked for: Camel Party Flight to Prague and Lissabon (Joost van Bellen, Benny Rodriquez, Michel de Heij, Roland Molendijk, Angelo), FFWD, Cosy Sessions, Islander ("Little louie" Vega, Kerri Chandler, Ian Pooley), Discotheque with Dimitri from Paris, Queensday Reunion Party (from '99 till 'now) A'dam, Clubrisk Winkel v. Sinkel, Party @ SBS6 DeBus, Wannahaves in the Powerzone (television program) and Linksaf (with Todd Terry). Besides playing in the clubs Youri Donatz is a regular guest on several radio stations; Dance distortion, Airpeace FM, RPLFM, ID&T, 3FM, and Fresh FM. One thing that Youri Donatz hopes to achieve in his career is to spin all over the world.
