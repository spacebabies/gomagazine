11 Years Kozzmozz in Vooruit

Eleven years down the line and Kozzmozz is still going strong! About 14 successful parties a year since we

But we haven’t forgotten all the joyful nights full of inspiring music : the atmosphere has never been better even though we still program the lesser known names and stay true to our original goal of promoting non-commercial electronic dance music ! When we do invite big names , they are the ones who are evolving with us and never without being accompanied by upcoming talents, both national and international.

For our 11th birthday, we bring you 5 rooms in which we have gathered three concepts, 8 international dj’s and live-acts and 11 national ones so that there’s something for everybody’s taste. The ones who have been there since the beginning and those who joined us later on our voyage through the musical universe
