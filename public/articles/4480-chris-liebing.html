Chris Liebing @ Ibiza

This summer 2006 Chris Liebing will launch his new night

It will be one of those nights that you will not want to miss!After two hugely successful seasons at Privilege, Chris will now take on his very own night with the aim of bringing something fresh and unique to Ibiza. His experience at Privilege over the last two years has shown him exactly what can be achieved on the island and how amazing the vibe can be when the right people are brought together. This new mindset has made him more determined and more focused than ever to bring something special to Ibiza and provide the people with a night that is unpretentious, inspiring and about sharing his passion for music.Over the last few years Chris has been able to observe many of the scenes new artists. Hand picked by Chris himself each resident represents a new and exciting force in the world of electronic music from around the globe. These are names you will come to hear a great deal about in the coming months! Joining the lineup will be a host of friends and surprise guests including two already confirmed shows with Collabs partner Speedy J. Sometimes these guests will be announced sometimes not depending on other commitments
