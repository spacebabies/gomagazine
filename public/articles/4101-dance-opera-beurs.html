Dance Opera beurs van berlage

Something special is going to take place in Amsterdam on October the 27th: The first ever Dance Opera will premiere in De Beurs van Berlage. A fascinating event merging the worlds of different music styles and different generations.

Inspired by dance composers, the classically educated (film) composer and pianist Laurens van Rooyen ( the Serengeti Symphony, Walt Disney) got the idea of composing a new kind of Opera based on the story of the familiar Greek hero Orfeo. The music was composed in collaboration with the dance producers Rank I and Geert Huinink, TiÃ«sto’s composer.

The international streetdance and skating group ISH will play the main part of the Opera. Well-known tv-actor Porgy Franssen will take the role of Mercuur, the messenger. This unique combination makes the Dance Opera Orfeo a contemporary and enchanting musical event.

The Imaginary Landscapes Foundation, the producer of the Dance Opera, will use part of the revenues of the premiere to provide musical instruments for children in former war zones. On the 27th of October the well known Dj Don Diablo will hand over a set of Dj equipment to Dragoljub Djuricic for the Pavarotti Music Center in Mostar.
