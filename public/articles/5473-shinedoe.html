Shinedoe

Shinedoe started performing as a dancer at an early age of 15. Speelt Saturday 06 October op KlanK # 2.

Shinedoe started performing as a dancer at an early age of 15.  Inspired by her African heritage and influenced by music from the early eighties, she performed at clubs & in video-clips (Quazar - Warp 9 - Ö)  Her mission at that time: "translating music through dance movements"

After performing as a dancer throughout Europe, Shinedoe decided to focus more on electronic music, at the time a mixture of US garage & Detroit techno/house.  Mixing music took a leading role in her life, resulting in her debut booking at the "VIP" club (Paradiso - Amsterdam) in 1995.

Ever since then, she's been invited by all the main festivals, clubs & parties in the Netherlands, and not much later, the first international gig was well underway.  

By 2004 Shinedoe decided it was time to turn a new page.  Her debut release on 100% Pure Records, "Dillema", turned into an immediate classic.  Being the Ibiza anthem of 2004, pushed by the likes of Sven Vath / Ricardo Villalobos / Luciano / Tobi Neumann just to name a few, was a dream start.  She continued the road of strong releases and as a follow-up got asked by numerous artists for remixes, going from The Knife to Booka Shade to Orlando Voorn.

2004 was also the birthyear of Intacto Records, by now already a highly acclaimed record label that Shinedoe runs together with Dylan Hermelijn (aka 2000 and One), concentrating on minimal yet uplifting Detroit techno funk.

2006 was a new turning point.  Shinedoe released her debut album 'Sound Travelling'.  The album combines Detroit techno with minimal techno, with a clear and present West African influenced rhythm section!

In 2007, Shinedoe releases her first mix-cd.  She was asked to follow the footsteps of Steve Bug / Joris Voorn / DJ Hell / ... in the series 'Fuse presents ...'  This mix cd reflects perfectly the style of Shinedoe: timeless tunes incorporating different flows & moods from Detroit & Chicago to Berlin.
