Ellen Allien

Ellen Allien ’ DJ, BPitch Control label founder, manager, producer. Speelt Thursday 03 July op 5 days off.

Allien siert al jarenlang, elke week, podia in alle noemenswaardige clubs en festivals wereldwijd, ondertussen heeft ze ook nog tijd om een eigen muziek- en fashionlabel te runnen. Eind 2007 kwam haar nieuwste track 'Go' uit. Deze multi-tasker weet van geen ophouden!

city: Berlin

style: hypnotic sequencies/ elechtech/ abstract

2004

Winter: Ellen Allien Remix Collection April: Ellen Allien Release Astral 12” BPC085 August: Ellen Allien – My Parade Mix-CD BPC094 Ellen Allien – Gold und Liebe / Out Of Control Remix for Yetti Ellen Allien – Laid Back Snack Attack Via RMX for George Thomson/Crosstownrebels

2003

Winter: studio work on the second album (scheduled release april 2003) Release of the second album “Berlinette” subsequent album tour “around the globe” March: Himmel Remix for Barbara Morgenstern / Label Monika May: Brasil-Tour with Laub Modeselektor trashscape mix August: Ellen Allien Koax Remix for Apparat Apparat fuse voc by Ellen Allien Lets get on remix for GoldChains The Way We have chosen remix for OMR Forms and Shapes remix for Sascha Funke September: Alles Sehen Rmxs Oktober: Chromosoma23 rmx by Ellen for Vicknoise/Factorcity rec. Japan-Tour/USA-Tour

2002 Release of the single “Erdbeermund’ ’ presentation of new musical approach; continuation of label parties in Berlin’s Tresor, Bastard, WMF; remix for Covenant Spring: two month DJ tour through Cananda, USA (NYC) and Japan (Tokyo) Summer: new party series “Boogy Bytes” in changing Berlin locations ’ focus on the presentation of current musical trends with an open ear for unkown styles; booking and coordination of Ellen Allien Fall: BPitch Control label tour “Gemeinsam’ with the release of an equally named compilation

2001 release of first album “Stadtkind” on BPitch Control; remix projects (for Malaria, Golden Boy & Miss Kittin, Peter Licht) further solo releases on BPC initiation of a new 7’-series label: “Spielwiese”

2000 studio work & label structuring; first Germany tour with BPitch Control

1999 founding of the equally named label “BPitch Conmltrol” in order to open yet another channel

1997 after the closing of Berlin’s E-Werk came the beginning of her own party series under the guise ‘Bpitch Control’‘ focus: futuristic sound, light and video mixing in changing industrial locations in order to fill gaps of Berlin nightlife with something

1996 release of own studio session on Braincandy

1995 Founding of first own label ‘Braincandy’; resident in the Berliner Tresor ’ every sunday ‘club the rest’ with DJ Tanith

1994 resident DJ at ‘Dubmission’ in Berlin’s E-Werk; project Loveparade: 60 hour ‘Braincandy’ -live transmission with interviews on Kiss FM directly from the Tresor; initiation and organization of Ellen Allien and Kiss FM

1993 worked in Berlin record store Delirium; first own radio show ‘braincandy’ on Kiss-FM Berlin

1992 DJ in the Bunker, Elektro, Tresor, Praxis Dr. McCoy Style: Abstract Techno

1991 first DJ-Job in Berlin’s Fischlabor ’ the most important techno spot in Berlin at the time where all well known Berlin DJ’s got their start

1990 the real techno-kick in Berlin’s Tresor

1989 contact to music of the future in Berlin: U.F.O. – the first house club in Berlin

1988 one year stay in London; first encounters with acid house in the Wag club.
