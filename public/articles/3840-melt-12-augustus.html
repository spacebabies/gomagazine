Melt 12 augustus 2005

12 / 08 / 2005—MELT @ COC, Leiden. MELT will have its highly anticipated third edition on Friday August the 12th!

This time we’ve invited the Subtronics crew to provide the evening with ‘smooth and sexy’ drum ‘n bass. As a very well known crew, they brought names like LTJ Bukem, MC Conrad, Photek,  Nu:tone and  London Elektricity  to Belgium & the Netherlands.

As Subtronics residents, MSC & D-Virus played along many of these big names. By bringing warm, musical and atmospheric tunes, they have an amazing ability to get people onto the dance floor. On the twelfth of August, this duo will perform an exclusive two-hour set!

Mar-ten joined the Subtronics crew recently, debuting with his performance on the Subtronics Stubnitz party with headliner Marcus Intalex. Among his favourite producers are Makoto, Calibre, Big Bud, Matrix and Logistics, so we can expect some quality tunes from this guy!

Sektor and Syze will be warming up and melt the audience before the headliners will take over. They also will be responsible for bringing the night to a suitable end.

We bring the goods… as long as you melt it!

Line-up:

22:00 – 23:15 Syze b2b Sektor [MELT]

23:15 – 00:30 Mar-ten [Subtronics]

00:30 – 02:30 MSC & D-virus [Subtronics]

02:30 – 03:00 Sektor b2b Syze [MELT]
