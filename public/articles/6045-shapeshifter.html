Shapeshifter

Chosen “best upcomming dj” at the dutch dnb awards 2002. Speelt Friday 22 August op Strictly 45's.

2002 was definetly the breakthrough year of dj shapeshifter.

After a year of residency @ the 070DNB parties, he got more and more bookings across Holland.

Shapeshifter’s record choice cannot be described easily as he tends to adept himself to the crowd he’s playin for, but it has to be said that he has a preference for the harder stuff. His mixing style ranges from tight to creative and if possible on 3 decks. With his mixing skills he managed to become upcoming dj #1 at the first national dnb-awards 2002 in Holland. Expect to see much more happening in the future since shape has succesfully finished the SAE audio-engineering course and is now beginning with producing.
