Steffi

De moederkloek van de vaderlandse electro-scene, dat is de Amsterdamse (via Eindhoven!) dj Steffi. Speelt donderdag 20 september op We Gaan Vreemd.

De moederkloek van de vaderlandse electro-scene, dat is de Amsterdamse (via Eindhoven!) dj Steffi. Samen met producer Dexter runt ze het toonaangevende Klakson-label, waar de afgelopen jaren platen van o.a. Duplex, Seymour Bits, Putsch ’79 en natuurlijk Dexter zelf zijn verschenen. Steffi houdt van een feestje en deinst er niet voor terug ze zelf te organiseren, denk bijvoorbeeld aan Elechicks, Lomotion, BoemTsjak, Soulrock en Vreemd en natuurlijk heeft ze ook op zo’n beetje alle grote festivals van de Benelux gedraaid. Plus op feesten ver daarbuiten. Inmiddels is electro een beetje een beperkt woordje om Steffie Doms recht te doen en wie weet mogen we dat binnenkort eindelijk eens horen op een eerste Steffi-plaat...

English: 

As a DJ, label boss, and club night promoter, Steffi has been straight slammin it in the Dutch dance scene since her emergence onto the scene in 1995. After moving from the south of Holland to Amsterdam in 1998 – Steffi wanted to start something that did not exist in Amsterdam and not even in Holland at that time – an all electro club night! And she did it with a feminine twist, calling it “Elechicks” and keeping it rockin’ all the while, playing from experimental electronic breaks to funky, pumping, uptempo disco and electro.

Not only at Club Mazzo as well as other venues in Holland like Melkweg, Paradiso, Doornroosje, Effenaar and 013 the concept was a big hit. On the night she showcases dutch atists like Dexter, Fastgraph, Legowelt, Cosmic Force, Rude 66, Funkarma, etc. as well as international ones like, DJ Assault, Godfather, Marco Passarani, Two Lone Swordsmen, Anthony Rother, Adult, etc.

In 2000 she took it to the next level and played at Hollands biggest popfestival Lowlands. Also that year Steffi and producer Dexter started their own recordlabel Klakson Records. They released a string of widely succesful records by Dexter, Fastgraph, Mesak, Duplex, Seymour Bits, etc.

Klakson records have been in international top ten lists by Dj’s such as Laurent Garnier, DJ Hell, Andrew Weatherall, The Advent, Miss Kitten, The Hacker etc., . Dave Clarke used a track from the first twelve on his last mix CD and labels like K7 Playgroup dj kicks), Mute, Ministry of Sound, City Rockers, Fabric, etc knocked on the door with compilation requests! Anno 2006 Klakson is a well known and highly respected label all over the world.

And Steffi is not slowing down- in 2001 she started another club night of minimal and eperimental house and techno, called Lomotion. She expanded her own DJ-ing to France, England, Italy and Germany and was also part of Captain Video’s Kurts brain project. Captain Video is well know as a solid VJ collective supporting DJ’s and acts trough the years like Leftfield, junky xl and many more.

In 2002 she played at the Dutch Awakenings Festival and one year later at Mysteryland and Extrema. Steffi also peformed on many big events and clubs like Speakerplay, Body to Body (Fuse Brussels), Kozzmozz (Vooruit, ghent), Output, Awakenings New Years party, Weekend (Berlin), Panoramabar (Berlin), Robert Johnson (Frankfuhrt) next to artists like Sven Väth, Laurent Garnier, Miss Kitten, Ellen Alien, Ricardo Villalobos, Adult., etc.

Now she’s a resident in Amsterdams most famous venue, Paradiso showcasing her own concept called chi, a night where only female dj’s, vj’s and mc perform. Also on a regular basis she organises her own parties in Pakhuis Wilhelmina and Studio 80 and plays at the Sugar Factory once a month.

You can check a rage of mixes for dutch radio/tv station VPRO on www.3voor12.nl. Expect a nice blend of electronic disco, dirty minimal house, acid and electro!
