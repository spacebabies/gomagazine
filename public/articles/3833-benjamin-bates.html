Benjamin Bates: new material coming up

Yes, there is new material coming up! A brand new 12” will be relased in the first week of august. It will contain two tracks > “The Manimal”and “16!”. I have put small clips of both tracks online, so you can check them out before the vinyl hits the shops.

If the sleeve turns out the way we intent, it will be art gallery material, but more on that soon. Of course, Marco’s new album ‘200V’ is also coming up and highly recommended. Release will be end of september! More details on his website: www.marcov.nl.
