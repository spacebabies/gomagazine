Benjamin Brown

In the 80’s, teen-Benjamin and friends were heavenly into rap, swingbeat and pop. Speelt Friday 28 September op Pure.

In the 80’s, teen-Benjamin and friends were heavenly into rap, swingbeat and pop. Later in the 90’s he was introduced to a New sound and another style called HOUSE. From this point, Benjamin’s love for all things electronic grew into a deep respect and passion. He decided to turn his life upside down and put the focus on his true passion, MUSIC. Bought his one and two that year and delivered a mix tape to a local called Havana. It took him just a short time to become Havana’s resident.

Sharing the decks with well-known Dutch DJ names and began to spread the sound that was in his heart to the crowd that visited this little club downtown.

Shortly after that; Benjamin got his first big break warming-up the crowd at SPEEDFREAX and JUICE in the ROXY. Heavy infected by the House-virus he also started promoting his own club nights at the ROXY in 1998 and introduced G-lounge 465 to the Netherlands.

From that time G-LOUNGE 465 and his production-company FUNKY PUPPIES were born. Determined to keep his head and sound in da underground he began to spread the vibe and his vision of house music to the people.

BenjaminBrown describes his style as percussive house with a sensual feeling. It’s utterly uplifting, it’ s fiery power forcing the listeners to forget their troubles, hit the dance-floor and dance.

Benjamin has been a steady force in the Dutch scene for a while now and has been invited to places as London, Miami, Casablanca, Curacao, Ibiza and Boekarest.

He joined the decks with international names as: Benny Benassy (subliminal), Jay Hannon (EBTG),Jory Hullkonnen( F-Com), Sebastiaan Leger (FR) and Mike Dunn (USA).

He also had the honor to perform at massive Dutch festivals like Dance Valley, Sensation White, Mysteryland(ID&T),Trance Energy(ID&T), N-JOY, Innercity (Brasil theme) and FFWD (LOVEPARADE of HOLLAND).

People can also hear him frequently at domestic venues as: Offcorso, NOW&WOW, Nighttown, Las Palmas, Mazzo, Hotel Arena, Escape, IT, Panama, Manhattan, Sinners, Nightlive, Winkel van Sinkel, etc, etc
