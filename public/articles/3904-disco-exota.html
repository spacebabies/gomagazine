Disco Exota, Zaterdag 20 augustus, Bootleg Dj Cafe

A monthly saturdaynight: addictive & adventureous! Resident dj’s Tako & Funknoir will invite 2 artists each month. This saturday it’s time for Kuczera and Pametex aka Melvin White aka That Nigger!!

dj’s Pametex (Clone, Frustrated Funk), Funknoir (Disco Exota), Kuczera (C-B-S) and Tako (C-B-S, Disco Exota).

Disco Exota??......’Exota’ stands for diversity and alienation, ‘Disco’ for adventureous mixing. Inspired by the legendary mixtapes from the eighties (Ron Hardy, Larry Levan, Daniele Baldelli & Tee Scott). They showed us, it all belongs together: disco, afro, funk acid, house, italo & electro 

Time: 21.00-06.00

Entrance: FREE

Address: Bootleg Dj Café, Mauritsweg 33, Rotterdam
