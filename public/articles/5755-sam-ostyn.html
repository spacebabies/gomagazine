Sam Ostyn

http://www. Speelt Saturday 23 February op Clink Label Night.

Sam Ostyn’s growing career is a symbiotic combination of producing and DJíing. Besides having played at Belgian’s finest clubs and events, his release schedule is expanding on a variety of internationally acclaimed record labels.

His love for music was passed on to him by his late father, who owned an eclectic vinyl collection, including many Motown – Stax and Atlantic records. By 1995 it was time for Sam to start his own search for early, electronic Detroit releases and contemporary techno. This lead to several dj-gigs in the Belgian nightlife alongside international dj’s such as Oxia, Deetron, Mark Broom, Renato Cohen and many more.

Having bought his first synth in 1996, he developed ëThe Cosmic Plantí into one of Belgium’s finest personal recording studios where he would experiment and consequently start working on his own productions.

At the turn of the millennium, Sam met Belgiumís leading techno lady, Trish Van Eynde. Having the same musical heroes inspired them to team up a year later, and ever since they have been working together. Their unique view on “Detroit-inspired future funk” techno was at the essence of their releases on “Chrome Resident Eps” and certainly on their debut “Open Window EP” on MB Elektronics. This did not go unnoticed as it attracted the attention of the likes of Derrick May, Carl Craig and Angel Molina to name a few.

An exclusive internet remix of Laurent Garnier’s “Controlling The House” reflected their love for melodic Detroit Techno and then the future looked very promising, to say the least.

Their alliance with Amil Khan & Charles Siegling for their Hong Kong based Technorient imprint seems to be the beginning of a long musical journey.

Sam Ostyn’s productions and mixing style unmistakably spreads lots of warmth. It is said to be techno filled with soul and passion. Call it “energetic high-tech funk” if you like, although all this categorizing is such nonsense. It’s his way of combining old and new waves that has proven to be a success on the dancefloor time after time. Techno with a (hi)story …
