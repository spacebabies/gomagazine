NEW SHAMEBOY SINGLE COMING UP

The work is finally paying off! If you've been to a SHAMEBOY gig lately, you've probably heard some of the new material.

 Their next single "Splend It" with B-side "Monofour" will be released at the beginning of May. It will be available on both iTunes and a simple vinyl maxi.


In the meantime, check out the SHAMEBOY remix of the Buscemi song "Sahib Balkan". Available on cd-single and iTunes!
