Ken Ishii, Sunriser 's album, out soon.

The new Ken Ishii's album is coming out in Europe/Us on april the 27th 2007. (E-Beat record/Pias)

Few collaborations appear on that CD, with Funk D'Void, 7th Gate, and....Fabrice Lig for the track "Organised green".

A 12'' is out now in Asia with a huge remix from 7th Gate...No info about a European release of that e.p.
