Alpha Twins

They call themselves “Alpha Twins” but despite this name the brothers Arjen and Anco are no twins at all.

Yet behind the turntables the two will strike you as being identical Twins, because of their similar taste in music and mutual agreement. Although Arjen and Anco were born and bred in the cheese town of Edam, Holland their pounding and banging hardstyle is far from “cheesy”.

The Alpha Twins started as residents at the Qlubtempo parties in Zaandam, but the “Qontact” party in the Heineken Music Hall in Amsterdam was their crucial test in front of a large group of people. Their first single “Contact me” wasn’t only the theme-song of that event, but the brothers were also programmed among celebreties like Judge Jules and Tiësto. In performances during DefQon1 festival, Qlimax, In Qontrol, Hard Bass, Mysteryland and X-Qlusive Arjen and Anco noisily showed that Alpha Twins is a synonym to rough hardstyle.

The brothers are full of ambitions and new ideas and therefore they thankfully accepted the chance offered by Q-dance. Nevertheless they do realize that the way to the top will be long and that there is a lot of work to be done if you want to make progress. A CD compilation like “Qlimax” is a passionate wish of the two brothers. Together with Dj Loca and Lay-c they are working on their music. As producers they want to optimize the reputation of the Alpha Twins as true partyraisers.
