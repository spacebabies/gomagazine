Paco Osuna

His passion for music started out when he was very young. Speelt Saturday 27 October op Monika Kruse.

His passion for music started out when he was very young. At the tender age of 14, he was part of a regular crowd at Studio 54, where the most carismatic DJ, Raul Orellana was spinning Acid House, marking Paco in the way of understanding DJing.

Date 3 In 94, Paco moved to Valencia, where he had his first contact with the decks. In 95, Paco made his first DJ set as a professional at the ACTV club, where he gained a one-year residency. After that, he started playing in many different clubs like Heaven, Universal, etc.

In the meantime, he worked in a record shop where he got access to a recording studio in wich he produced three singles for the label Lucas Records.

In 99, he got a residency at the Amnesia Ibiza, where he had the brillant opportunity to be lined up with the biggest DJs and could learn the different styles in order to build his own.

The Amnesia was also the place where Paco got in touch with the person that influenced his future equally personally as well as profesionally: Sven Vath. He soon discoverd Paco to be very talented and included him in his Cocoon Booking agency, so that Paco was the first Spanish DJ to play for this prestigious agency.

In 2001, Paco decided to leave house music and to move backwards closer to Techno and from now on, Paco´s career went better and better, leading him to the best clubs and festivals all over the world and giving him a ¨seat¨ in the techno scene.
