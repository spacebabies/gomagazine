New EevoNext by The Moderator (and Estroe remix)

Once again The Moderator delivers another fine release with his trademark melodic techno. On the third release of digital EevoNext label he brings four tracks ranging from Electro, IDM to full-on Detroit techno.

EEVONEXT presents the 'Kingdom of Catbu EP' by The Moderator

 

The EP starts of with The Secrets of Catbu, a driving classic techno track with bombastic strings for some dancefloor craze. Darkwater is an emotional and melodic electro track while Cold returns to the dance floor with a more modern cut. Cold gets the remix treatment from DJ Estroe who transforms it into a deep and warm minimal techno monster. The EP ends with fuzzy strings and dreamy melodies with the off-beat Dragon hunters.

Track listing (click to download demo versions):

1. Secrets of Catbu
2. Darkwater
3. Cold
4. Cold Estroe remix
5. Dragon hunters

Additional information

EevoNext is a fully digital web label emerging from the fundaments of Eevo Lute Muzique, one of the most influential techno labels from the Netherlands. Started in November 2006, it currently brings a bi-monthly digital release of a featured artist with emphasis on quality and playability. Available from the EEVONEXT.COM web store as high quality MP3 and as MP3/AAC/WAV from established online stores like iTunes, Beatport, Juno and others.
