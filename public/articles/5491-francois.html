DJ Francois was born on 28th of july 1969 in the Netherlands. His interest in house music began back in 1989, since the he plays his own style with success. He played on several big party’s the last years. He is now the famous afterparty man from the Netherlands in his afterclub zino. He is also a resident DJ on the famous sexual Crazyland party’sÂ 

In the beginning the people didn’t quite understand this electronic music, but after a while the dance floor was filled up with party people and for most of them house music now was born!

Because house music is a music-style that goes on and on and lasts all night, Francois decided to start an Afterclub in the south of the Netherlands. In 1994 the club opened his doors on a Sunday morning at 6:00 a.m. and the first party in The Limit had started. During the next years the Sunday mornings @fterclub became very popular and people from all over the Netherlands & Belgium found there way to Hilvarenbeek to have fun or finish their night out , now it is at @fterclub ZINO!! nr1 Afterclub.
