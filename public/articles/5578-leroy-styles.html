Leroy Styles

DJ Leroy Styles komt van originele Rotterdamse bodem en staat voor Groove, Electro en Funky Techno maar noemt het zelf liever gewoon House. Speelt Saturday 08 December op F*ckers.

DJ Leroy Styles komt van originele Rotterdamse bodem en staat voor Groove, Electro en Funky Techno maar noemt het zelf liever gewoon House. Leroy Styles begon in 2003 met zijn grote doorbraak. Met zijn onnavolgbare platensmaak variërend van rauwe rampestampers tot extaseverhogende vuige electro op het juiste moment, werd hij al snel gevraagd voor de heetste housetoestanden in de Rotterdamse clubscène. Leroy Styles predikt Rotterdam to the bone maar slaat zijn vleugels uit over Nederland.

Leroy Styles weet precies wat hij wil en heeft de laatste jaren een sterke opmars gemaakt in binnenlandse- en buitenlandse dance scene met zijn eigen producties ‘Jus Bounce’, ‘Booty Bass’, ‘Frodo’ en ‘Rise’. Deze platen zijn niet alleen klappers op de Nederlandse dansvloer maar krijgen ook buiten onze landsgrenzen erkenning van dj’s zoals Laurent Garnier, Jack de Marseille, Sander Kleinenberg, Rene Amesz, Steve Angello en vele anderen.

Leroy is geliefd voor zijn groovende housesound waarbij hij zijn publiek net even wat verder de diepte mee in neemt. Op sommige momenten neigt zijn sound zelfs naar minimal of techno. Als je Leroy er echter zelf naar vraagt zal hij antwoorden dat hij “gewoon house” draait. Geen praatjesmaker dus, deze Rotterdammer. Zijn performance slaat aan want Leroy is een veel geboekte dj, in toonaangevende clubs zoals The Thalia Lounge, De Rechter, Players en The Powerzone. In het verleden draaide hij veel in de Now&Wow. Tijdens de FFWD dance Parade in Rotterdam dit jaar, was hij gastheer op zijn eigen wagen; The Thalia presenteerde de Leroy Styles truck.
 

English biography:

LEROY STYLES

Leroy Styles stands for groove, minimal and funky techno or as he likes to call it “just house”. Leroy fell in love with house music in the early 90’s and made his first steps at mixing in 1995. Back then he was working with cassette tapes because he couldn’t afford regular turntables. In 2001 Leroy Styles bought his first records and started practising on regular turntables together with his childhood friends. A couple months later he bought his own turntables and in 2002 he was given the opportunity to perform at an R&B club.
But Leroy soon realised that the urban crowd did not feel his house music so it was not what he was looking for. A year later Leroy got his first big break when he got the chance to show his skills at club Off-Corso in Rotterdam, one of Holland’s finest clubs. From then on things started moving fast for Leroy.

Soon Leroy became a resident DJ at the clubnight Superstarz and after that he appeared regularly for the always critical crowd at The Thalia Lounge.
The big break for Leroy came when he was asked to prove himself at one of the biggest and hottest clubs of The Netherlands: Now&Wow. Only after a few gigs, he became a resident DJ and later on he also became the musical promoter of Now&Wow.

Besides DJ-ing Leroy hosted his own club nights (Hufters, Electro Rockers) and since September 2006 he started with his own club night: LOOKS at The Thalia. Every last Saturday of the month he invites the best national and international DJ’s like Benny Rodrigues, Brian S, René Amesz, Kenny Dope (USA), Dennis Ferrer (USA), D-Ramirez (UK), Jazzy Jeff (USA) and many more.

Meanwhile Leroy performs all over The Netherlands at the best clubs like Panama (Amsterdam), The Thalia (Rotterdam), De Rechter (Eindhoven), Escape (Amsterdam), Jimmy Woo (Amsterdam), Now & Wow (Rotterdam), The Level (Arnhem), Off-Corso (Rotterdam) and many more. He also plays his music outside this little country for example South America and France.

Nowadays you can find Leroy spending his free time in the studio where he is working hard on his first releases. ‘Booty Bass (dream)’ is already ‘the summer sound’ in The Netherlands and causes madness on the dance floor. His first release ‘Jus Bounce’ is now gaining huge recognition and is played al over the world by DJ’s such as Jack de Marseille, Sander Kleinenberg, D-Ramirez, Rene Amesz, Benny Rodrigues, Steve Angello and many more.
Superstar D-Ramirez mixed the ‘Sessions 13’ mix on UK super-label Ministry of Sound and included Leroy Styles’ Jus Bounce!!!  This track is released on 25 Hertz Recordings, which is launched by Rene Amesz in December 2006.
