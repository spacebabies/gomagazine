Closer to the Plastikman

Dat Richie Hawtin-Plastikman-een van de meest innovatieve technoproducers is, moet je onderhand wel weten. Maar welke soft-en hardware gebruikt hij nou precies en hoe is het allemaal met elkaar verbonden?

‘Richie is using Ableton Live and his new CTRL controller as the front-end to Derivative’s TouchMixer, which is generating live visuals for over 25 Plastikman songs. Playing a music loop in Ableton also automatically triggers and syncs a visual element in TouchMixer, while volume faders and other sliders affect details of the visual elements, like brightness or size. This setup enables Richie to experiment with visual combinations, which as a side effect, induces unique sonic combination.’
