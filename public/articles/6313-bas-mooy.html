Bas Mooy

comingRotterdam home-based dj/producer Bas Mooy (1976) has been organizing techno-parties for over five years now. Speelt Saturday 21 February op From techno 2 techno.

Solo, under the flag of Exposure of Sound and in co-operation with established Dutch organizations such as Strictly Techno, Sound Architecture and Teknation. In 2003 he became a resident at Compound, a one year project at Rotterdam’s main underground club, Waterfront and recently he joined the Strictly Techno collective, techno-pioneers known for their legendary underground parties in Rotterdam for over six years now. Besides that he is also hosting a monthly evening in Bootleg, providing a stage for upcoming talent and locals.

In 2002 he started the well-respected label Audio Assault Recordings, of which releases are played by many of the top jocks around the world. Together with labelpartner Jeroen Liebregts he recently started up a second label called Arms. These days he’s spending much time in the studio, breeding on new material and future releases. Bas is working four days a week at the main Dutch techno distributor Triple Vision, which is the exlusive home of worldwide successful labels such as Planet Rhythm, Heroes, Compound, Mankind, Skunkworks, illegal Stockholm, Highland Beats and Audio Assault.

As a dj he proved himself behind the decks of established venues such as Nighttown, De Effenaar, Doornroosje, Waterfront, Calypso, De Blauwe Aanslag, Bazart and Cinerama. Besides that he also played gigs in Belgium and Sweden. His style is best described as pounding techno-music, firm and with one major keyword: energy!
