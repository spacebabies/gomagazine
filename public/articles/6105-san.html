San

When you like a dj and producer that plays and writes music with lots of energy, breakbeat or 4/4, aggressive basslines and lots of percussion, SAN suites the job perfectly. Speelt Saturday 20 September op Summerlake.

When you like a dj and producer that plays and writes music with lots of energy, breakbeat or 4/4, aggressive basslines and lots of percussion, SAN suites the job perfectly.

SAN’s productions have graced the turntables and mix cds of top DJs around the globe either under his own name, ‘SAN’, or his “Solid Sessions” and “SanProject ” monikers. Tracks such as “Janeiro”, “Sunday” and “Mechanical People” have been heavily supported by all the big dj’s. His original productions have been featured on mix cds by djs such as Sasha, John Digweed, Armin Van Buuren, Paul Van Dyk, Dave Seaman, Deep Dish, Ferry Corsten and Jimmy Van M.

His productions have been remixed by big name producers such as; Armin Van Buuren, Rui da Silva, James Holden, Lemon 8, Chris Cowie, Marc et Claude and even Alice Deejay.

Now the time has come for SAN to showcase his diversity and wide selection of musical taste by releasing his first ever solo album! Expect a combination of break beat, ambient, tech house, a flavor of techno and some trancy influences by the end of the year.

Although a couple of the tracks on the album have been released and gotten him supporters that vary from the range of John Digweed to Pete Tong to Timo Maas to Paul Oakenfold, there’s way more to expect from this man, considered by many to be the next big thing. Keep a close eye on him, cause he might catch you by surprise.

His latest single called ‘Sunday’ and on the other side ‘Mechanical People’ is a perfect example and has been causing quite a stir within the dance industry. Sunday, a tech house monster of a track with a gritty bassline is totally different from the flipside: Mechanical People, a breakbeat-floor-detonater, which could very well have been something the Chemical Brothers would have come up with
