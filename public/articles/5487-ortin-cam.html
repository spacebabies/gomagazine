Ortin Cam

It all started on the 8th of April 1982. Speelt Saturday 24 November op Awakenings.

It all started on the 8th of April 1982. Tom Macoye was born and raised in a small town called Heusden-Zolder, Belgium.

At the age of 12 he started to buy his first dance music cds and records and on the age of 14 he started spinning records in the attic. His life slowly changes when the Belgian radioshow “Technoville” is launched and from that moment on he’s a total techno freak. A year later at the age of 15 he starts making his first beats and at the age of 17 he gets the chance to play at a local radio in Brussels. The programmer really likes the tracks Tom has made, so Tom passed the demo to a few labels in Belgium and one of them is Headroom Music.

The Headroom crew likes the demo so much that it gets released on the Invasion Imprint and at the age of 17 the first 12” is in the stores. After a few more 12”s for Headroom they split up and Tom starts producing for a few other labels as well such as Pornographic, Anlx, Highland Beats. But since he and Zzino are still working closely together, Tom keeps on releasing on the Ex-Headroom Imprints now handled by NEWS.

Also on a musical level Tom and Zzino get along fine and they decide to work together as a production team. Guss Carver as well joins the team and releases follow on Invasion, Session, Corachi, Traction, MB Elektronics, ...

Tom is one of the guys to look out for in the future; at the age of 22 he has already touched down on over 20 labels such as Global Trax, Giant and Dwarf, Highland Beats, Equator, Bound, Soul Access, Invasion, Session, MB Elektronics, Traction, Pornographic, Traction, In-Cycle,... He has remixed people like Adam Jay, Tomaz vs Filterheadz, Zzino, Jan Liefhebber, Panic Attack, ... and soon he’ll kick ass around the globe with a banging live set..
