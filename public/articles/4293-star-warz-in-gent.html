Star Warz in Gent

Star Warz remains the leading Drum

We hope to be able to do it all over again this time with another one of those leading Drum ‘n’ Bass labels : Technique Recordings.

Uk Midlands based Technique Recordings was established in 1999 by Simon ‘bassline’ Smith and Andy Wright of Drumsound in order to provide a fresh vehicle for their joint / individual musical projects.

The close alliance between the two parties began at the ‘Technique’ club night (one of the biggest eclectic break beat nights) in their home town of Derby in 1998. Since then this alliance has been the defining strength behind the label combining experience, enthusiasm and knowledge in order to progress as a crew rather than as a set of individuals.

Technique Recordings are set to make their presence felt with their mission to promote the name of their label, establish their sound in order to build a flavor that is devastating on any dance floor world wide. They will do this with a world tour supporting the new ‘Technique Recordings compilation’ (more info below).

And off course, Star Warz is Belgium was a desired place to help establish this mission.

They also bring DJ Fresh – of former Bad Company fame – the man behind Breakbeat Kaos, collaborator with Adam F and the web-site Dogs on Acid.

Combine these sets with the voices of MC Biggie & Yougman and you know you’re in for a treat !

Residents Millennium Kru will top it off and Saiko of Hydra will warm up the place. Be there or be square !
