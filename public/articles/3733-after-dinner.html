After Dinner event in Supperclub

Magma will collaborate from the 2nd of June with Supperclub (Jonge Roelensteeg / Amsterdam) with several residents to host an after dinner/shopping event. From 9 pm till Midnight Angelos, 100% Isis, Egbert Jan Weber & Carlos Valdés are hosting every Thursday night and evening.

Come join us for the opening on the 2nd of June.

Eggie (Egbert Jan Weber) & Carlos Valdés are hosting the 9th of June from 9 pm till midnight. From 11 pm till midnight dj’s are welcome to play their newest records, fresh from the vinylprinter.
