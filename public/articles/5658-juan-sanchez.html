Juan Sanchez

Like many talented artists Juan developed an interest in music at a very young age and played in several rock bands. Speelt Saturday 19 January op Ableton Live.

But it was when he studied near Detroit that he fell in love with techno music and started deejaying. He discovered that taking his guitar into the dj booth gave him the thrills he was looking for. Adding special effects and outstanding guitar solo’s while looping beats and spinning records he created an unique sound in the techno scene. His creativity, experience and special talent makes him an outstanding performing artist, capable of rocking dance floors worldwide.

Style Juan plays a blend of energetic but warm and funky techno or tech-house and creatively twists it with his outstanding live electric guitar performance with special effects and loops.

National-highlights Mysteryland (Europe’s oldest dance festival!), Free Your Mind, Rockit, Booch and many more. Well known techno events like Teqnology and Integrate and many other hot spots like Paard van Troje, Winkel van Sinkel, De Rechter, Poema. Several live broadcastings were recorded for National Radio 3FM, VPRO’s 3voor12 and ID&T Radio.

International-highlights Juan regularly plays in Spain in club like la Sala del Cell in Girona and in clubs in and around Barcelona. He also started making name in ‘techno-country’ Belgium at the popular Metro Techno events. He recently visited Russia for a big event with Steve Lawler in St. Petersburg.

Played-with Juan Sanchez has been scheduled with deejays like Funk D’Void (UK), Derrick May (US), Alexander Kowalski (G), Jori Hulkkonen, Steve Lawler (UK), Miss Yeti, Joris Voorn (NL), The Advent (UK), Christian Wunsch (Esp), Oscar Mulero (Esp), John Acquaviva (CAN), Secret Cinema (NL), Acid Junkies (NL), Jack de Marseille (F), Damon Wild (US), Bart Skills (NL) and many more.

Discography Juan will release his first record in 2006. Expect a new sound with an unique twist.
