Fred Nasen

Fred Nasen started his dj career in the early 90’s with residencies at the (in)famous ‘At The Villa’ parties , Balmoral after club (Ghent) and the H20 club. Speelt zaterdag 5 juli op Beats of Love.

Playing clubby house, he immediatedly became a well in demand dj. Later he secured himself a job at the News record- and distribution company, where he’s working now for 9 years as a label & import manager.

From clubby house he soon evolved to an explicit tech-house style inspired by the London scène with The End from Mr. C at the forefront.

After releasing his own productions (together with studio partner Dimitri De Wever (Swirl People)) on the ‘Local’ imprint , Fred started up his own label ‘Visitor’ around 1999 ,on which the first release was his ‘Sunday visitor ep’ which lays between funky techno & the real chicago house , his second release with remixes by nathan coles was featured in Carl Cox top 10 chart in Muzik magazine

After that he signed releases by Gideon Jackson, Big Hair, Eddie Richards,Lilac Road Corporations, Gareth Oxby, Colin Dale ,Grant Dell , Dj Ali, Asad Rizvi , Tom Gillieron , Ravi Mcarthur & his own productions !

As such he is the only dj/ label manager who represents the real tech house scene in benelux

He played already at clubs abroad like Pacha ( Buenos Aires , Argentina) ,The End (London), Surreal party (Brighton), Folie’s Pigalle, Le Wagg & Scream parties ( Paris), Malta, La Salla del Cell (Barcelona), Impulz & Pinkpop (Holland), De Rechter & Off Corso (Rotterdam), Nord Digital (Lille), Otv club (Zagreb/Croatia) , Macedonia , Sarajevo (Bosnia) ,Seven Eleven (Atlanta) , Miami , ...

In Belgium he played on the big clubs & festivals like I love Techno 2004 Flanders Expo 2004 , House Torhout, Dour Festival (2x) , Axion Beach Rock (3x), 10 Days of Techno, Bobby's Mission, Axion Tek Night Ancienne Belgique, Food club , Fuse , Culture Club , La Rocca, Cafe d’Anvers, Pukkelpop 2002 with Xpress 2 , Seb Fontaine & Mr C., ...

Fred was also resident at the monthly Studiotek parties together with Dave Mothersole where they had guests like Mark Farrina, Richard Grey, Gideon & Darren Roach. Already 5 years he is resident at the famous kozzmozz / Muzzic parties in Ghent!!

His favourite producers & dj’s are Pure Science , Craig Richards , Michael Mayer , D’julz , Tobi Neumann , Ravi Mcarthur & more.
