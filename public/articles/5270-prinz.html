Prinz

DJ Prinz is al jaar en dag resident voor Café d'Anvers, waar hij wekelijks het publiek verwent met zijn soulful house sets.

Dj Prinz staat ook gekend om zijn steeds opgewekt humeur en zijn kleurrijke voorkomen. Het samenspel tussen zijn sets en zijn ingesteldheid leveren de ideale combinatie om op ieders gezicht een brede glimlach te toveren!

He’s a resident in Cafe d’Anvers and regularly plays Supperclub in Amsterdam, Now & Wow in Rotterdam, Portugal and these days also more and more in Moskow. A lot of summers you could find him behind the decks in Ibiza at Dance valley in Amnesia, Km. 5 or Pacha.

He’s mixed one of the two cd’s for Cafe d’Anvers’ 10th anniversary compilation for which he also produced ‘This is my Sound’. These days he’s involved in a new Belgian tech-house label with the name LUM Recordings, a label run by Nico Lombardi. More info: www.lumrecordings.com.

A big smile comes to your face when you think of this colorful Belgian character. One can expect a mixture of garage, deep and tech-house tunes that will entertain and be enjoyed by everybody on the dance floor. During his first gigs between 1995 and 1997 Prinz played all the major clubs in Belgium. Café d’Anvers, La Rocca, Food Club, Who’s Who’s Land, Red&Blue, Tour & Taxi, Rêve d’O to name but a few.

Since 2000 Prinz is no longer only a DJ. Prinz started producing and running his label “Butterfly Records”. Next to his resident club nights at Café D’Anvers, Now & Wow and Supperclub (Holland) he remains very active abroad. He plays in Moscou, Tel Aviv (Tamarz Lounge), Amsterdam (Chemistry & More), Ibiza (Amnesia & Space), Paris (Rex), Barcelona (Race-Dingue) and festivals like Impulz and Dance Valley in Holland.

As a producer Prinz has already a few releases behind his name on labels like Diversion in Belgium, Piglife Records in France and of course on his own Butterfly Records. Prinz is an experienced and talented DJ who rocks dance-floors with ease.
