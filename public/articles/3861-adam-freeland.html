Adam Freeland releases sneakers

Adam Freeland has been working with Etnies over the past 2 years developing our own very limited edition shoe.There will only be very limited 50 pairs available.Info will go up on the Marine Parade Website very soon as to how you can be one of the lucky 50.New releases

New tracks upcoming from the Evils, plus new signings Sly Fi and Stone Lions. There’s also some new tuneage from the man himself Mr Freeland, with re-mixes from our own Evil Nine and young german electro head Daniel Taylor, so watch out for them! Finally, we’re hearing someone called the Plump DJs are reworking an Evil Nine track (possibly Devil Stuff), so we sit and wait with our fingers crossed…

Adam has just finished compiling and mixing the latest in the legendary ‘Back 2 Mine” series, featuring some of his favourite artists such as …And You Will Know Us By The Trail of Dead, Autolux, Interpol, TV On The Radio, Boards Of Canada, PJ Harvey, and many more… It really is something special, a total excursion from what he is know for.In his own words: “Its been thoroughly enjoyable to be able to put together a lot of my favorite artists from the world beyond the dance floor on a mix. Its based on a mix tape I did for a friend of all my favorite tracks that I’m listening to at home right now.”’Back 2 Mine’ will be avail in late July through the Marine Parade website and good record stores.
