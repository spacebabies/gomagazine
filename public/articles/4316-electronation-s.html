Electronation's DJ Mason @ Zeezicht Breda

Zaterdag 28 januari: Electronation’s DJ Mason in Zeezicht Breda Aanvang: 21.30 Entree gratis Support: DJ Cris2Four

Venue: ZEEZICHT Breda Adres: Ridderstraat 1 Grote Markt Breda Web: http://www.cafezeezicht.nl

Bio Mason

Mason is rapidly becoming a star in the international dance scene. At the young age of 24, this talent has already accomplished a lot. He performs all over the world, integrating his electric violin during his energetic dj-sets. Mason is a producer of many music styles and recently received a prestigious price for young artists from the commissioner of the Dutch Queen. He is a multi-talented artist who is unstoppable, and for sure someone to keep an eye on!

At this moment Mason is working hard at becoming an international dj star. Mason’s bookings takes him to Asia (Taiwan, Hong Kong, China, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Jakarta), but also from Russia to Israël and from Finland to the Caribbean. The highlight of 2004 was when #1 DJ of the world Tiësto personally invited him to play violin with him in the dj booth at all 3 sold out Tiësto in Concerts. Totally over 75.000 people concluded that Mason was one of the highlights of the amazing concert of Tiësto. Earlier that year Mason was the support act at the world tour of Tiësto at a huge event in Lima, Peru. But also in his home country The Netherlands this youngster tours around with his record case and played with dj’s like Ferry Corsten, Don Diablo, James Holden, Dave Clarke and Armin Van Buuren. As a resident of ‘Electronation’ he played at Holland’s nr.1 event ‘Dance Valley’. In addition, festivals Impulz Outdoor, Extrema Festival, Parkpop, Beachpop, as well as all the main clubs in the Netherlands have had Mason performing on their stages once. With a lot of ambition and future plans, you need to keep an eye at this young talent.
