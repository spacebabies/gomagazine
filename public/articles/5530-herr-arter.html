Herr Arter

The upcoming electrosound of the late nineties made Arter addicted to this ‘new’ sound. Speelt Friday 16 November op Freak 'N' Chic label night.

The upcoming electrosound of the late nineties made Arter addicted to this ‘new’ sound. He started buying records in 2001 and soon all his friends also turned into ‘electro-junkies’. In his search for an electroscene in Amsterdam he came in touch with Electronation. While Roy organised the parties, Arter got the chance to create the typical Electronation sound with Terry Toner. 2003 was the year of success for Arter, as a resident of Electronation parties in Amuse, Melkweg, Winston en the rest of Holland.
