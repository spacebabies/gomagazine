Hitmeister D

Hitmeister D is zeer waarschijnlijk een van de weinige echte belichamingen van de UrbanEclectische sound van Nederland. Speelt donderdag 06 september op Riot.

Hitmeister D is zeer waarschijnlijk een van de weinige echte belichamingen van de UrbanEclectische sound van Nederland. Met een hoog tempo draait hij een mix van vette Discotracks, sexy Electro, Hiphop, Garage, House classics, R&B en Funk. Hitmeister doet dit met zoveel finesse en power dat het geen dansvloer onberoerd laat.

English:

Hitmeister D started out like everybody else with a passion for music. Not one specific style of music, no specific genre, just MUSIC. In 1999 he started to spin for various different crowds in clubs in Rotterdam. But it wasn’t until�?� that his career took a sharp turn when he got his first booking in Holland’s number 1 club: The NOW & WOW (www.Now-Wow.com)

This was the beginning of a new era in dance land and the Hitmeister wanted to contribute. With a mix of fat discotracks, sexy electro, hiphop, garage, houseclassics, R&B and back to Funk, Hitmeister formed his own style. Some call it ‘eclectic’, some call it ‘urban’ but regardless of the label it is given, his reputation travels beyond the local clubs.

In the last 4 years, Hitmeister D has built a solid and highly regarded record of clubs and events throughout Holland. Besides programming and playing @ two of the FFWD Trucks this year and the After Stage he is booked for more huge festivals like Mysteryland, Extrema Solar, Luxurush and the 10 Year Anniversary of Loveland. These are just a minor portion of his bookings that will show you what he is capable of. Besides the festivals he has a streak of fashion events and openings to his name for none other than big multinationals like Diesel, Marlies Deckers, G-Star, Replay and off course the International Fashion Week in Amsterdam & Barcelona. Today he holds his residency spots at Jimmy Woo in Amsterdam and Now&Wow in Rotterdam and can be found all over the country. On top of all of this Hitmeister D was voted “Best Urban Eclectic and Breaks DJ”; in 2003 next to renowned world legends as DJ Tiesto and Michel de Heij.

Off course internationally his sound is also greatly appreciated as has been seen in the Culture Club in Belgium and in various clubs in Istanbul where he can be found from time to time.

Hitmeister D also known as Deniz is what we call a night crawler, dedicated promoter and a regular party freak.
