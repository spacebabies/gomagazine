Sidney Samson

Begon op zijn veertiende met het draaien van R&B en Hip-Hop om volledig over te stappen naar housemuziek in 1999. Speelt Friday 13 February op Shake & Pop.

Hij brak door naar het grote publiek toen hij in 2003 de resident DJ werd van de Exxellent avonden in de The Matrixx. Is inmiddels één van Nederlands meest geboekte DJ’s en bekend om zijn warme, altijd energieke sets.

Als producer scoorde Sidney Samson vorig jaar nog een grote hit met It’s All Funked Up en daarvoor als Don & Johnson met de clubknaller Bring That Beat Back.

Met recht één van de meest geboekte DJ’s van dit moment. Als resident DJ van The Matrixx leerde het Nederlandse publiek hem pas echt kennen met zijn unieke sound.

As a seven year old boy Sidney started playing the guitar. From there on he knew that he wanted to make music for the rest of his life. After seeing a couple of DMC mix championships his interests were going towards dj-ing.

He started dj-ing in 1995 at the age of 14. During that time he mainly played RnB and HipHop tunes in the local clubs. In 1999 he switched his style completely over into housemusic. ??In 2002 Sidney had his first clubhit called Don and Johnson-Bring That Beat Back (Digidance). This track ended up high in the club charts.

In 2003 Sidney became the resident dj of the Exxellent nights at one of Hollands biggest club The Matrixx. In that same year he came with a club banger called Sidney Samson-It s all funked up! (Spinnin Records ) which had a high rotation by djs like Erick E and Jean.

At the end of 2004 Sidney had a huge clubhit named Nobody Move (Work). In February 2005 Sidney had his first Latin release on the label Fresh Fruit. This track is called: Santa Domingo-La Fiesta. With his groovy new sound Sidney is going to rock the clubs! So keep an eye on this dj and look out for his productions!
