Steve Carta

It was almost 15 years ago when Steve first discovered how to mix different styles of music whilst working behind a bar in Jersey. Speelt Saturday 24 January op Rebellion.

He learnt the importance of what good music could do to change the atmosphere.

Steve moved in 1993 to Amsterdam to maintain his career in the music industrie. He played every night in several club and bars, wich gave him a more professional attitude torch club owners and bookers.

Slowly he played at some of Amsterdam’s more established clubs, like the Ministry and Sinners in heaven. In 2001 Steve’s first appearance at Amsterdam’s mega trendy lounge the Supperclub was a fact and he went from there. Now in 2003 he performs for DanceValley and is a resident every other month at HQ.
