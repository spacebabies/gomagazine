TOP 100 electronic records of all times

In the month of July, Electronation will celebrate its 3 year anniversary party with a few different concepts. After 3 years of parties and great music, we have decided to do an Electronation chart of the top 100 electronic records of all times.

The Electronation top 100 chart is a personal chart that reflects your taste and not a specific genre, just like the Electronation events which are very wide and varied. That’s why it is not just the electro top 100, but the Electronation top 100. The chart will include the complete spectrum of electronic music from industrial to pop and from italodisco to minimalhouse. If it?s electronic and you like it, you can vote for it in your own top 10 of all times.

We will collect all of the entries and based on your votes, we will compile the total top 100. Send your top ten to [email protected] before Friday the 15th of July and stay tuned to the radio show and the website. The chart will be played during the Electronation radio show on Saturday the 23rd of July from the KINK FM studio, which will be situated on that night in the Paradiso, where the official 3 year anniversary party of Electronation will be held.

If you are looking for inspiration for what kind of music to vote on, you can always check the playlists of past Electronation radio shows on www.beatcast.nl or check the section of past parties on www.electronation.nl to see some of the artists we have worked with. Also, all of the information on the chart and the 3 year anniversary events will be available through our website and our Electronation radio show every Saturday between 20.00 and 22.00 on KINK FM.

The official 3 year anniversary party of Electronation will be held in the Paradiso on the 23rd of July from 0.00 until 05.00. For the 3 year anniversary party, we have decided to show three of the different Electronation concept in the different rooms. In the Main Room, we have invited some of the Electronation friends to DJ, who have supported us through the years such as Don Diablo, Laidback Luke, Steve Rachmad, our residents Dion and Terry Toner and Herr Arter and many more. The visuals will be brought by 310K. In the small room, we will have the entire Electronation DJ crew, partying in the traditional Electronation style with DJ’s Groove Addicts, Pablo & Nauta, Generik, Eva Maria,Nobody Beats the Drum LIVE and many more. To make the evening complete, we have de Kijkbuiskinderen as visual support for the small room.

The basement will also be part of the evening, but information on that will be announced later. On the night itself, the Electronation radio show will be hosted from the Paradiso; a live broadcast with interviews, charts and much more.
