Marco Bailey

Marco Bailey is gekend om zijn energieke technosets die over de hele wereld op veel gejuich onthaald worden. Speelt Saturday 24 May op Marco Bailey.

Op zijn label MB Selektions vind je een grote verscheidenheid van elektronische muziek; van minimal tot electro. Marco Bailey sleepte al meermaals de trofee van beste Belgische DJ in de wacht.

Na meer dan tien jaar hard werk als producer en deejay, heeft Belg Marco Bailey nu een niveau bereikt waar hij zelf tevreden mee is. En hoe hij daar komt? Door opoffering en toewijding, zoals hij het zelf omschrijft.

Al zijn toewijding leidt ertoe dat Carl Cox hem persoonlijk vraagt een plaat te releasen op zijn nieuwe label In-Tec Records. Vele releases verder op labels als In-Tec, Primate en Zync, mocht Bailey een compleet album uitbrengen op het vermaarde Primate.

Tegen het eind van het jaar 2000 werd Pornographic Records geboren, dat hij samen met vriend Christian Varela oprichtte. Op dit label verschijnen, naast eigen werk, ook producties van Umek, Tim Baker en Oxia.

English biography:

Its a burning, all consuming drive that can only be calmed down by getting into the music. Its an urge that luckily gets a positive outcome in the studio when Im working on a track just as long as it takes to get it to sound as it does in my head, or when Im puzzling on my dj-set until it releases the exact energy that makes the crowd lose it. I can best describe this burning, consuming urge as a positive disorder. Plainly positive because it gives a meaning to my life Belgians number one techno artist, is hitting hard on the back. He is about to embark on a world tour to promote his music, so he is seriously putting some graft into craft. His sets are usual top of the night big room sets, and is precisely the reason why he has been elevated to the first string of Techno Electro Minimal dj/Producer. Baileys innovation and reluctancy to conform to a particular genre of music is booth refreshing and unique. Awarded 3 times in a row as best Belgium Techno Dj. “No nonsense, just great music. Techno is pure energy. Giving yourself one hundred percent to make people feel good. Keep an open mind and you’ll get the proper respect.” With the release of his album ” Rude Boy” on May 17th (2004) and the following live & dj tour, Marco Bailey once again jumps ahead of the competition. Fifteen years of sacrifice and dedication have seen Belgian’s number one dj break through the national borders to join the big league of worldwide techno artists. This didn’t go unnoticed by leading man Carl Cox who asked him to sign onto his new label Intec Rec twice. Killer tracks on in-tec, Primate, Zync and Tortured, confirmed his strong reputation as a producer over the last eight years. In return he set up MB Elektronics & MB Selektions to release quality music and remixes by the skills of adam beyer, umek, ben sims, claude young, cristian varela, redhead, axel karakasis, takaaki itoh… In 2000, he joined forces with Spanish pride Cristian Varela to form Pornographic Recordings, a respected outlet for musicians as diverse as Umek, Gayle San, Oxia etc.. He is a selfmade man with a mission to entertain people with the love of pure techno music. And that’s exactly what he has been doing all these years: travelling from west to east back again, igniting dancefloors from Rio to Tokyo with his unique blend of underground sounds, funky techno and from Time to Time some nice minimal or Teck House sounds…even some great Boards of canada tunes as you can check on the mix compilation 160 min. of marco bailey or his latest mix cd PositiveDisorder..
