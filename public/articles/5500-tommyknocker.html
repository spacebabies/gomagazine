Tommyknocker

Real Name: Tommaso Marra Profile: Tommyknocker starts his careen in 1994 when he begins performing in some ‘illegal’ parties organized in Rome. Speelt Saturday 24 November op Multigroove.

Real Name: Tommaso Marra Profile: Tommyknocker starts his careen in 1994 when he begins performing in some ‘illegal’ parties organized in Rome.

From ‘95 to ‘97 he collaborates with the ‘Virus staff’ of Freddy K, and is Resident Dj of some disco like “06” and after “Oxygen” (the first hardcore club of Rome) .

He even takes part to a radio show called “Hardcore Play” where he works as director together with Freddy. from ‘97 to ‘99 he plays in the biggest hardcore parties of Rome.

Then finally he dicedes to begin his carrer as producer and buys some stuff ! His first E.P. ‘The Realm’ has been released on Traxtorm Records in the year 1999.

In 2000 he sets up a new hardcore team called T-Factor with 2 other traxtorm artists: Impulse Factory and Erick-S.X. So a new E.P. arrives in the stores: ‘How Many More Out There?’ ! thanks these productions he can play at many many parties worldwide ! he travels to Canada, Holland, Switzerland and germany and performs at parties like : Megarave, Streetparade & Energy, Goliath, Army of Hardcore.
