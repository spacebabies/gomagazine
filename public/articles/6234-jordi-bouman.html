Jordi Bouman

Although Jordi Bouman is still a pretty young artist, he can already speak of a promising career and a decent past as artist/DJ. Speelt Wednesday 31 December op NYE & Afterparty Twisted Stereo.

Definitely one of the next generation DJ’s such as James Zabiela and James Holden!

It all started at the age of 14 when Jordi bought his first set of turntables to take his musical desire’s in own control.

After practicing a lot, Jordi developed his own style en technique.

Nowadays Jordi is a big supporter of playing with cd’s, which you will notice while he’s playing because about 90% of his set is done with cd’s.

Jordi: “Besides the fact that I can put more creativity into my music, I can also take more and newer music to a gig.

A very important aspect about Jordi is that he doesn’t uses his capability’s with the cd players to let everybody show what they can, but always thinks about the music first.

Jordi: “It’s all about the music and not about the tricks”

The style that Jordi is playing can not easily been put into corners, or easily be described. A lot of people will call it progressive, but he uses influences from a variety of other styles.

You can catch him flirting with rock, house, electro and breaks. But on the other side he also uses influences from disco, tech-house and even pop.

That his style is picked up by the crowd can be noticed by the number of gigs that come in and the quality events where Jordi is playing.

Last few months he shared the decks with Sander Kleinenberg, James Holden, Slam!, Silicone Soul, Tom Stephan, Fred Numf, Paul van Dijk, Remy, Luci?n Foort, Roog, Victor Coral and Stef Vrolijk with his 16 Bit Lolita’s (just to name a few). On club nights like Sander Kleinenberg and Stef Vrolijk’s own “Everybody”, James Holden’s Border Community night, the official Mysteryland after-party from ID&T and off course the always progressive Exposure nights.

Besides that Jordi is playing in clubs all over the country, he isn’t a foreign stranger anymore.

This summer he played in Ibiza and it looks that on this island is a lot more going to happen for him next year! What else could you ask for, on still nr.1 Party Island in the world!
