Stonebridge to mix and compile Dance Valley 2005 album

Producer, remixer and DJ extraordinaire StoneBridge to mix and compile Dance Valley’s 2005 ‘Sunrise’ album. Expect a double CD of the sexiest house music seamlessly blended into the ultimate summer 2005 soundtrack. On august 5, 6 and 7, a new landmark is set for Dance Valley Festival; the first and only weekender of the ‘Woodstock of Dance’ will take place boasting a lineup of top international DJ’s and an unbelievably enthusiastic audience  putting Dance Valley firmly on the world’s yearly festival agenda.

StoneBridge has just finished remixing David Morales new single ‘Feels Good’ on Ministry Of Sound, Missy Elliot’s new single ‘Lose Control’ on Atlantic USA and Jenn Cuneta’s ‘Come Rain Come Shine’ on Ultra Records USA.
