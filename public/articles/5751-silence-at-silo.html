Silence at Silo Demo Contest

Studio Brussel / Switch & Silenced det up the national producer & dj contest.  Enter the contest and win a spot for Silenced at Silo (September 27th)!

The 2008 jury will hold members of all important Belgian clubs/party promoters combined with Poppunt, Je m'en Fish, News, Out Soon, and Focus Knack.

Besides a spot on the line-up of Silence at Silo, you can already fantasize about a release on News, your track on the Switch compilation, interview in Knack Focus/OutSoon/Switch, and much much more ...

What to do???

- only cd is accepted
- min 30 minutes
(also producers have to make a mix of 30min of their own tracks)
- tracklisting
- mention phone number, address, & e-mail
- short bio
- electronic music, all genres!
- enter the contest between 25 jan - mai 17

Switch/Silenced dj/producers contest
Populierenlaan 8
3210 Linden
Belgium
